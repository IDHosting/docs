---
title: "Download Backup of Home Directory, MySQL, or Email Only"
description: "Learn how to create and download specific backups of your website's home directory, MySQL databases, or email accounts using cPanel."
---

Backing up your website data regularly is crucial for security and disaster recovery. This guide will walk you through the process of downloading specific backup components, such as your home directory, MySQL databases, or email data, using cPanel's Backup Wizard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Begin by logging into your cPanel account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Backup Wizard">
    Once logged in, locate the **Files** section and click on the **Backup Wizard** icon.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/backupwizard-icon.gif" 
        alt="Backup Wizard icon in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Backup">
    Under the **Backup or Restore** section, click on the **Back Up** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Partial Backup">
    In the **Select Partial Backup** area, you will see various options for creating and downloading specific backups. To download a backup of your **Home Directory**, click on the **Home Directory** option.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/partial-backup.gif" 
        alt="Selecting Home Directory for partial backup"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Backup Download">
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Click on **Home Directory** again to confirm your selection and initiate the download.

    The download process will begin automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You can follow similar steps for backing up MySQL databases or email data by selecting the respective options in the "Select Partial Backup" section.
</Note>
