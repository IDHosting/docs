---
title: "Generate and Download a Full Backup of Your cPanel Account"
description: "Learn how to create and download a complete backup of your cPanel account to ensure your website data is safe."
---

Backing up your cPanel account regularly is crucial for website safety. This guide will walk you through the process of generating and downloading a full backup of your website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel account by navigating to your cPanel login URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Backup tool">
    Once logged in, locate the **Files** section and click on the **Backup** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/backup-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Backup Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate a Full Website Backup">
    Under the **Full Backup** section, click on the **Download a Full Website backup** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/download-button-full-backup.gif" 
        alt="Download Full Website Backup Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Backup Settings">
    In the configuration options:
    <ul>
      <li>Select <strong>Home Directory</strong> as the backup destination from the drop-down menu.</li>
      <li>Enter your email address to receive a notification once the backup process is complete.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/generate-backup-config.gif" 
        alt="Backup Configuration Settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    <p>Leave the <strong>Include integration links</strong> option as it is.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Your Backup">
    After the backup generation is finished, you will receive an email notification. You can then download your full account backup by returning to the **Backup** section and navigating to **Download a Full Account Backup**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/backup-download-complete.gif" 
        alt="Backup Download Complete Notification"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Regularly backing up your cPanel account is essential for disaster recovery and data protection. Schedule these backups to ensure your website is always protected.</Note>
