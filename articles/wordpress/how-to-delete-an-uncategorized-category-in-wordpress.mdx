---
title: "Delete an Uncategorized Category in WordPress"
description: "Learn how to manage the default 'Uncategorized' category in WordPress, including renaming it as it cannot be deleted."
---

<Note>
  The default 'Uncategorized' category in WordPress cannot be deleted. However, you can rename it to better suit your website's organization.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your WordPress Dashboard">
    Access your WordPress admin area by navigating to your website's login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Categories">
    Once logged in, go to **Posts** in the left-hand dashboard menu, and then click on **Categories**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-dashboard-category-option.gif"
        alt="WordPress dashboard showing Posts > Categories option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the Uncategorized Category">
    Locate the **Uncategorized** category in the list. Hover your mouse over it and click on the **Quick Edit** option that appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-uncategorized-category-remove.gif"
        alt="Quick Edit option for the Uncategorized category"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename and Update">
    In the fields that appear, change the **Name** and **Slug** for the category to your desired values. Once you've made your changes, click the **Update Category** button to save them.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-uncategorized-category-update.gif"
        alt="Renaming the Uncategorized category and updating"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

The 'Uncategorized' category has now been successfully renamed.
