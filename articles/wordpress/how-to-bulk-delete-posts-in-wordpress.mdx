---
title: "Bulk Delete Posts in WordPress"
description: "Learn how to efficiently delete multiple posts at once in your WordPress site."
---

You can delete posts in bulk in WordPress, including selecting and deleting specific posts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to WordPress">
    Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to All Posts">
    From the Dashboard menu, go to **Posts** and click on **All Posts**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-first-time-all-post.gif"
        alt="WordPress All Posts screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Posts for Deletion">
    Check the top checkbox to select all posts displayed on the current page.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-removing-multiple-post.gif"
        alt="Selecting all posts in WordPress"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Bulk Action">
    Click on **Bulk Actions**, select **Move to Trash** from the dropdown menu, and then click **Apply**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/move-to-trash-wp-multiple.gif"
        alt="Applying Move to Trash bulk action in WordPress"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your selected posts will be moved to the **Trash**. To delete them permanently, navigate to **Trash** and click **Empty Trash**.

You have now successfully learned how to bulk delete posts in WordPress.
