---
title: "Write and Publish Your First Blog Post in WordPress"
description: "A guide on how to write and publish your first blog post using WordPress."
---

Writing your first blog post in WordPress is an exciting step. Follow these instructions to publish your first post.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to WordPress">
    Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Add New Post">
    From the **Dashboard** menu, go to **Posts** and click on **Add New**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-ft-newpost.gif"
        alt="WordPress Add New Post screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Title and Content">
    Enter your post title and write your blog post content in the editor.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/writing-first-blog-post.gif"
        alt="WordPress post editor with title and content fields"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Tags">
    Under the **Post** tab, click on **Tags**. Type relevant tags for your post and press **Enter**. You can add multiple tags.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-adding-post-tag.gif"
        alt="WordPress post tags section"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Category">
    From the **Categories** section, select the appropriate category for your post. You can also click on **Add Category** to create a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish Your Post">
    Click the **Close (X)** icon next to the category selection, then click the **Publish** button located at the top of the page.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/wp-publish-first-post.gif"
        alt="WordPress publish post button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Congratulations on writing and publishing your first blog post in WordPress!
