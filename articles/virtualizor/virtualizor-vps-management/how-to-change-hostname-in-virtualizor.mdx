---
title: "Change Hostname in Virtualizor"
description: "A guide on how to change the hostname of your VPS within the Virtualizor control panel."
---

You can easily change the hostname of your Virtualizor VPS by following these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Access your Virtualizor Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side navigation panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the row corresponding to the VPS you wish to manage. The row will highlight. Click on the **Wrench icon** that appears on the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor click manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Hostname">
    In the VPS management interface, navigate to the **Settings** tab. Select the **Change Hostname** option. Enter your desired new hostname in the provided field and then click the **Change Hostname** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-change-hostname.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor change hostname interface"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reboot VPS">
    To ensure the hostname change is applied correctly, **reboot** your VPS.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your hostname has now been successfully updated in Virtualizor. You can proceed to use the new hostname for your server.
