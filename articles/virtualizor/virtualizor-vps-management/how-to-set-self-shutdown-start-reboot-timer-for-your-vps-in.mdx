title: "Set Self Shutdown/Start/Reboot Timer for Your VPS in Virtualizor"

---

You can set a self-timer for your VPS to start, shut down, reboot, or power off at a specified time using Virtualizor's convenient feature.

## Setting a Self Shutdown/Start/Reboot Timer

Follow these steps to configure a scheduled action for your VPS:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Access your Virtualizor Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the desired VPS row until it is highlighted. Then, click on the **Wrench icon** that appears on the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor click manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Self Shut Down">
    In the management options, click on **Self Shut Down**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-self-shutdown-icon.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Self Shut Down icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Timer and Action">
    Enter the desired **Date**, **Hour**, and **Minute** for the action. Then, select the desired **Action** (e.g., Shut Down, Start, Reboot). Finally, click on **Submit**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-self-shutdown-time-option.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor self shutdown time and action options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your timer has now been set. Your VPS will automatically perform the selected action at the scheduled time.
