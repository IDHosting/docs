---
title: "Change the VPS Root/Admin Password in Virtualizor"
description: "Learn how to change the root/admin password for your VPS using the Virtualizor control panel."
---

You can change the root/admin password of your VPS operating system using Virtualizor.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Log in to your Virtualizor Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the appropriate VPS. The line will highlight. Click the **Wrench icon** on the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor click manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Click on **Settings** and then choose **Change Password**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-settings-password.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor settings change password option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Enter your new password in the provided field and confirm it. Then, click **Change Password**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-change-password-root-admin.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor change password form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Power Off VPS">
    On the top right side of the panel, click **Poweroff**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-force-shut-down.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor power off button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start VPS">
    Finally, click the **Start** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-start-vps-icon.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor start VPS button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

It may take a few minutes for the VPS to start. You can then access it with your new password.
