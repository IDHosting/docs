---
title: "Change the VNC Password in Virtualizor"
description: "This guide explains how to change the VNC password for your VPS within the Virtualizor control panel."
---

You can change the VNC password for your Virtualizor VPS by following these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Access your Virtualizor Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    On the left-hand side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the row of the VPS you wish to manage. The row will highlight. Click on the **Manage icon** that appears in the highlighted row.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor Manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access VNC Settings">
    Within the VPS management area, click on **Settings** and then select **VNC**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-vnc-password-option.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor VNC password settings option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change VNC Password">
    Enter your desired new VNC password in the provided field. Confirm the new password by entering it again. Click the **Change VNC Password** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-change-vnc-password-final.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor change VNC password confirmation button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reboot VPS">
    **Reboot** your VPS for the changes to take effect. You can now access VNC using your newly set password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

We hope you found this guide helpful for changing your VNC password in Virtualizor.
