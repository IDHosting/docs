---
title: "Enable/Disable APIC, ACPI, VNC in Virtualizor"
description: "A guide on how to enable or disable VNC, APIC, and ACPI for your VPS within the Virtualizor control panel."
---

Virtualizor allows you to easily enable or disable VNC, APIC, and ACPI for your virtual private servers (VPS). Follow these steps to manage these settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Access your Virtualizor Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    On the left-hand side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Locate the VPS you wish to configure. Hover your mouse over the VPS row until it highlights, then click the **Wrench icon** that appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor click manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access VPS Configuration">
    In the VPS management area, click on the **Settings** tab. From the dropdown or sub-menu, select **VPS Configuration**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-settings-tab.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Settings tab and VPS Configuration option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure System Settings">
    Under the **System Configuration** section, you will find options for VNC, ACPI, and APIC.
    *   To **enable VNC**, tick the checkbox next to **VNC**.
    *   Similarly, tick the checkboxes for **ACPI** and **APIC** to enable them.
    *   To disable, simply uncheck the respective boxes.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-enable-disable-vnc-acpi-apic.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor VNC, ACPI, APIC enable/disable options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes and Reboot">
    After making your desired changes, click the **Submit** button to save them. You will then need to **reboot your VPS** for the changes to take effect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

We hope you found this tutorial helpful in managing your VPS settings within Virtualizor.
