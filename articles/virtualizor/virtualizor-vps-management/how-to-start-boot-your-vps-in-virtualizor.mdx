---
title: "Start/Boot Your VPS in Virtualizor"
description: "Learn how to start or boot your Virtualizor VPS if it is currently offline."
---

If your VPS is down or offline, you can easily start it using the Virtualizor control panel. Follow these steps to get your VPS back online.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Access your Virtualizor control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    Once logged in, click on **List VPS** from the left-side panel.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the row corresponding to the VPS you wish to manage. The row will highlight. Click on the **Wrench icon** that appears on the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor click manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the VPS">
    In the VPS management interface, locate and click the **Start icon** on the top right side.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-start-vps-icon.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor start VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  It may take a few minutes for your VPS to fully start after initiating the process.
</Note>
