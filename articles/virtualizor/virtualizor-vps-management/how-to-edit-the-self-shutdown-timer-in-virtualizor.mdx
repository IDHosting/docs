---
title: "Edit the Self-Shutdown Timer in Virtualizor"
description: "Learn how to edit the self-shutdown timer for your Virtualizor VPS."
---

You can easily edit the self-shutdown timer in Virtualizor by following the steps below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Log in to your Virtualizor Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the desired VPS. The line will highlight. Click on the **Wrench icon** on the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor click manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Self Shut Down">
    Click on the **Self Shut Down** option.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-self-shutdown-icon.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor Self Shut Down icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Timer">
    If you have multiple timers, hover your mouse over the one you wish to edit. The line will highlight. Click on **Edit** on the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-edit-self-shutdown-timer.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor edit self shutdown timer"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Make the necessary changes to the timer settings and then click on **Edit** to save them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The self-shutdown timer has now been updated. Your VPS will adhere to the new schedule you have configured.
