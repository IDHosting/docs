---
title: "Reinstall an OS Using the Virtualizor Automatic OS-Reinstaller"
description: "A guide on how to reinstall the operating system on your Virtualizor VPS."
---

<Note>This process will destroy all data on your VPS. Ensure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Log in to your Virtualizor Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the desired VPS. The line will highlight. Click the **Wrench icon** on the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Click Manage VPS"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Reinstall OS">
    Click on the **Install** tab, then click on **Reinstall OS**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-install-tab.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Install Tab"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Operating System">
    Under **Reinstall OS**, select the desired operating system. If multiple versions are available, choose the appropriate one.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-select-os.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Select OS"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Root Password">
    In the password field, enter your desired root password or click the **key icon** to generate a random one. Save this password in a secure location. Then, click **Reinstall**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-reinstall-os-password.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Reinstall OS Password"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Reinstallation">
    A confirmation box will appear. Click **OK** to proceed with the OS reinstallation.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-data-loss-warning.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Data Loss Warning"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>OS reinstallation typically takes between 5 to 10 minutes to complete.</Info>
