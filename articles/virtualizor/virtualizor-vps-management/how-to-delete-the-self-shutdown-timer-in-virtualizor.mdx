---
title: "Delete the Self-Shutdown Timer in Virtualizor"
description: "This guide explains how to remove a self-shutdown timer for your Virtualizor VPS."
---

You can remove the self-shutdown timer in Virtualizor by following the steps below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Log in to your Virtualizor Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the appropriate VPS. The line will be highlighted. Click on the **Wrench icon** in the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor click manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Self Shut Down">
    Click on the **Self Shut Down** option.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-self-shutdown-icon.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Self Shut Down icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Timer">
    If you have multiple timers, hover your mouse over the one you wish to delete. The line will be highlighted. Click the **Delete** button in the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-delete-self-shutdown-timer.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor delete self-shutdown timer button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    When prompted, confirm the deletion by clicking **OK**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The self-shutdown timer has now been successfully deleted.
