---
title: "Access VPS via VNC in Virtualizor"
description: "A guide on how to access your Virtualizor VPS using VNC, useful for emergency situations or OS installations."
---

You can access your VPS via VNC in an emergency, such as when a firewall blocks your IP, the internet connection on the VPS is not working, and you cannot access SSH. Also, you can use VNC for OS installation/reinstallation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Virtualizor Control Panel">
    Navigate to your Virtualizor control panel and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage VPS">
    Hover your mouse over the desired VPS. The line will be highlighted. Click on the **Wrench icon** in the highlighted line.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor manage VPS icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access VNC Icon">
    On the top-right side of the VPS management page, click on the **VNC icon**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-vnc-icon.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor VNC icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch VNC">
    A new popup box will appear. Click on **Launch VNC**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-vnc-choose-option.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor VNC launch option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="VNC Area">
    The VNC area will open in a new window, allowing you to interact with your VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-html5-vnc-area.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor HTML5 VNC area"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

We hope this tutorial is helpful to you.
