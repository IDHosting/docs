You can find the Control Panel URL, username, and password in your VPS welcome email.

**Virtualizor Login details example:**
*   **Control Panel URL:** `https://cp.example.com:4083` or with the IP Address.
*   **Username:** Your email address.
*   **Password:** `example`.

## How to log in to Virtualizor Control Panel?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open VPS Control Panel">
    Navigate to the Control Panel URL provided in your welcome email. This URL might look like `https://cp.example.com:4083` or use an IP address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Enter your Virtualizor **Username** (your email address) and **Password** in the respective fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login">
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-login-page.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor login page with username and password fields"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    Click on the **Login** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After clicking **Login**, you will be redirected to the Virtualizor VPS management area where you can manage your virtual private server.
