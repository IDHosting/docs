---
title: "Change Your Virtualizor Account Password"
description: "Learn how to change your Virtualizor account password to enhance security."
---

If you can access your Virtualizor VPS control panel, you can easily change your Virtualizor password. We recommend changing your password frequently and using a strong one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Virtualizor Control Panel">
    Access your Virtualizor control panel by navigating to its URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to My Profile">
    From the top right-hand side of the dashboard, click on your **Profile Avatar** and then select **My Profile** from the dropdown menu.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-account-password.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Control Panel - Profile Avatar location"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Your Password">
    Click on the **Account Password** tab. Enter your desired new password in the provided field, and then click the **Change Password** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-change-password.gif"
        alt="Virtualizor Account Password change interface"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your Virtualizor account password has now been successfully changed. You can log in to your control panel using your new password.

<Tip>For enhanced security, choose a password that includes a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.</Tip>
