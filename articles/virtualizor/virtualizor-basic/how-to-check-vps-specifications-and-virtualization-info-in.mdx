---
title: "Check VPS Specifications and Virtualization Info in Virtualizor"
description: "Learn how to check your VPS specifications, including RAM, disk space, virtualization type, IP address, and resource usage within the Virtualizor Control Panel."
---

You can check your VPS specifications such as RAM, disk space, virtualization, IP Address, and more directly from the Virtualizor Control Panel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor">
    Begin by logging into your Virtualizor Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Allocated Resources">
    Under the **Information** column, you will see the allocated RAM, CPU cores, disk space, and bandwidth for your VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-check-details-of-vps.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor VPS details showing allocated resources"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Resource Usage Details">
    To view the resource usage details of your VPS, click on the **Wrench Icon** located next to your VPS entry.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-click-manage-vps.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor Wrench Icon to manage VPS"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Management Page">
    On the **Management** page, you can find detailed information including the VPS IP address, current disk usage, bandwidth consumption, and other relevant specifications.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-vps-full-details.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor VPS management page with full details"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

This guide should help you easily check your VPS specifications within Virtualizor.
