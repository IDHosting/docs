---
title: "Check VPS Online or Offline Status in Virtualizor"
description: "Learn how to check the online or offline status of your Virtualizor VPS through the control panel."
---

You can easily check your Virtualizor VPS status to determine if it's online or offline by following these steps.

## Checking VPS Status

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Virtualizor Control Panel">
    Access your Virtualizor Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to List VPS">
    From the left-side panel, click on **List VPS**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-list-vps-menu.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor List VPS menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View VPS Status">
    Under the **Status** column, you will see whether your VPS is **Online** or **Offline**. If it shows **Online**, your VPS is running and functioning correctly.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/virtualizor-online-offline-status.gif" 
        alt="Virtualizor VPS Online/Offline status indicator"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If the status doesn't seem up-to-date, you can click the **Refresh icon** next to the **Status** option to update your VPS status.
</Tip>
