---
title: "Check Your Automatic Subscriptions in PayPal"
description: "Learn how to easily manage and view your recurring payments by checking your automatic subscriptions in PayPal."
---

This guide will walk you through the steps to check your automatic subscriptions in PayPal, giving you better control over your recurring payments.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your PayPal account">
    Navigate to the PayPal website and log in using your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Once logged in, click on the **settings** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-settings-icon.gif"
        alt="PayPal settings icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Automatic Payments">
    Go to the **Payments** tab and then click on **Automatic payments**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-automatic-payments-button.gif"
        alt="PayPal automatic payments button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Active Subscriptions">
    Ensure **Show Active** is selected. If you have any active subscriptions, they will be listed here. If you have multiple subscriptions, click on **See More** to view all of them.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-subscription-list.gif"
        alt="PayPal subscription list"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these steps, you can effectively manage your automatic subscriptions and maintain full control over your recurring payments in PayPal.
