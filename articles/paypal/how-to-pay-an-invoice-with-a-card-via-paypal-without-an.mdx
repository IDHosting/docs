PayPal Card Payments allow you to make payments directly using debit or credit cards. You can also use alternative options if your card is not supported by PayPal.

## Paying Your Invoice

Follow these steps to pay your invoice using a credit or debit card via PayPal:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your hosting client area">
    Access your account by logging in to your hosting client area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the invoice section">
    Go to the **invoice section** and select the unpaid invoice you wish to pay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Credit/Debit Card and Pay Now">
    Choose the **Credit/Debit Card** option as your payment method and click the **Pay Now** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Card Details and Submit Payment">
    Enter your debit or credit card details, billing address, and any other required information. Click **Submit Payment**.

      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-pay-via-debit-or-credit-card.gif"
        alt="PayPal payment screen for entering card details"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  After submitting your payment, you may see a confirmation that the payment has been made. However, you might be prompted to verify your card by entering an OTP (One-Time Password) provided by your card issuer. Once this verification is complete, the payment will be fully processed.
</Note>
