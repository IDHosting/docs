---
title: "Cancel an Automatic PayPal Subscription"
description: "Learn how to cancel pre-approved, automatic payments or subscriptions directly from your PayPal account."
---

You can cancel pre-approved, automatic payments or subscriptions within your PayPal account. Follow these steps to ensure you avoid future charges.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your PayPal account">
    Access your PayPal account by entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Once logged in, click on the **settings** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-settings-icon.gif"
        alt="PayPal settings icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Automatic Payments">
    Go to the **Payments** menu and select **Automatic payments**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-automatic-payments-button.gif"
        alt="PayPal automatic payments button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Subscription">
    Ensure **Show Active** is selected. Any active subscriptions will be displayed. If you have multiple subscriptions, click **See More** to view them all.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-subscription-list.gif"
        alt="PayPal subscription list"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel the Subscription">
    Click on the merchant's store name to view the full subscription details. Then, click the **Cancel** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-cancel-subscription-click-on-cancel.gif"
        alt="PayPal cancel subscription - click on cancel button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    A confirmation message will appear. Click **Cancel Automatic Payments** to finalize the cancellation.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-subscription-final.gif"
        alt="PayPal subscription final confirmation"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these steps, you have successfully canceled your automatic PayPal subscription and will no longer be charged.
