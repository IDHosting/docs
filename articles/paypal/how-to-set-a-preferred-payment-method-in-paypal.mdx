---
title: "Set a Preferred Payment Method in PayPal"
description: "Learn how to set your preferred payment method in PayPal when you have multiple cards linked to your account."
---

You can easily set a preferred payment method in PayPal if you have multiple debit or credit cards linked to your account. This ensures that PayPal uses your chosen card by default for transactions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your PayPal account">
    Navigate to the PayPal website and log in using your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Wallet">
    On the top menu, click on the **Wallet** option.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-click-on-wallet-menu.gif"
        alt="PayPal wallet menu option highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your preferred card">
    From the left-hand side menu, click on the card you wish to set as your preferred payment method.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-remove-card-select.gif"
        alt="Selecting a card from the list in PayPal wallet"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as preferred">
    Locate the option **Set as preferred** and click on it.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-set-as-preferred.gif"
        alt="Set as preferred button highlighted for a PayPal card"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully set your preferred payment method. PayPal will use this card automatically for future payments unless you choose a different method at checkout.
