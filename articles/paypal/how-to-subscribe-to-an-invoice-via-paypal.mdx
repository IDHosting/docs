---
title: "Subscribe to an Invoice via PayPal"
description: "Learn how to subscribe to an invoice for recurring payments using PayPal."
---

You can subscribe to an invoice for recurring payments. This option is available if you are purchasing a recurring service and the invoice due date is in the future.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your hosting client area">
    Access your hosting client area by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Invoices and select an unpaid invoice">
    Go to the **Invoices section**. Open the **unpaid invoice** you wish to subscribe to. Ensure that **PayPal** is selected as the payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate PayPal Payment">
    Click on the **PayPal** or **Pay Now** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-subscribe-button.gif" 
        alt="PayPal Subscribe Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your PayPal account">
    To link your PayPal account, choose **Pay with PayPal**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-link-account.gif" 
        alt="Link PayPal Account"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to PayPal">
    PayPal will prompt you for your login details. Enter your PayPal credentials and click **Log in**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-login-to.gif" 
        alt="PayPal Login"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select payment source and confirm">
    Choose a suitable payment source, such as your debit card or another preferred method. Then, click **Agree & Pay Now**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-subscription-payment-source.gif" 
        alt="PayPal Subscription Payment Source"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Payment">
    Finally, click **Submit Payment**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-subscribe-accept.gif" 
        alt="PayPal Subscribe Accept"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

If the process is successful, you will receive a notification confirming the payment and the creation of your subscription. If you see a **Return to Merchant** link, click on it. If it doesn't work, you can safely close the browser window.
