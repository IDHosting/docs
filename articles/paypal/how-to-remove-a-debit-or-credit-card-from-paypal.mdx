---
title: "Remove a Debit or Credit Card From PayPal"
description: "Follow this tutorial to remove your Debit or Credit Card from your PayPal account."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your PayPal account">
    Navigate to the PayPal website and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Wallet">
    On the top menu, click on the **Wallet** option.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-click-on-wallet-menu.gif" 
        alt="PayPal wallet menu option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the card to remove">
    From the left-hand side of the Wallet page, click on the debit or credit card you wish to remove.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-remove-card-select.gif" 
        alt="Select card to remove from PayPal"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate card removal">
    Scroll down the card details page and click on the **Remove Card** link.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/paypal-remove-card-link.gif" 
        alt="Click remove card link in PayPal"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm removal">
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Click **Yes** or **Remove This Card** to finalize the removal.

    <Note>Once removed, a confirmation message will be displayed on your screen.</Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
