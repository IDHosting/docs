---
title: "Link a Debit or Credit Card to Your PayPal Account"
description: "Follow this tutorial to link your Debit or Credit Card to your PayPal Account."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your PayPal Account">
    Log in to your existing PayPal account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Wallet">
    On the top menu, click on **Wallet**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-click-on-wallet-menu.gif"
        alt="PayPal wallet menu option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link a Card">
    Click on **Link a card**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-link-card.gif"
        alt="PayPal link a card button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Card Type">
    Click on **Debit or Credit Card**, or any other appropriate option shown in your PayPal account.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-link-card-debit-or-credit-card-option.gif"
        alt="PayPal select debit or credit card option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Card Details">
    Enter your card details and then click on **Link Card**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paypal-link-card-enter-details.gif"
        alt="PayPal enter card details form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Card Verification">
    It may prompt you for card verification, such as a one-time password or other available verification methods. After you complete it, your card will be added successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have successfully linked your card to PayPal. You can now use it for quick and secure payments.
