---
title: "Reset cPanel Account Password"
description: "Learn how to reset your cPanel account password if you have current access to your account."
---

If you have access to your cPanel account, follow these steps to reset your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Log in to your cPanel account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Password & Security">
    In the **Preferences** section, click on **Password & Security**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/password-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Password & Security Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Passwords">
    Enter your current password in the **Old Password** field. Then, enter your new password in the **New Password** field, or use the password generator to create a strong password.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cp-security-change-password.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Change Password Interface"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Password Change">
    Click on **Change your password now**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If a success message is displayed, your password has been changed. You can now log in to your account using your new password.

<Note>
  If you have forgotten your cPanel password and cannot access your account, please open a support ticket with us for assistance.
</Note>
