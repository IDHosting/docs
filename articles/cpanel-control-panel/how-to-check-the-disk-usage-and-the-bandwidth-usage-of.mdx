title: "Check Disk and Bandwidth Usage of Directories"
description: "Learn how to monitor your disk space and bandwidth usage within cPanel to identify directories consuming significant resources."
---

If you're receiving disk space warnings from cPanel and need to pinpoint which directories are using the most space, this guide will help you. We'll also cover how to check your bandwidth usage.

## Check Disk Usage

This section will guide you through checking your disk space usage in cPanel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Access your cPanel account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Disk Usage">
    In the **Files** section, click on the **Disk Usage** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/diskspace-usage-icon.gif" 
        alt="Disk Usage icon in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Disk Space Summary">
    The Disk Space Usage area will display which directories are consuming the most space. You can also check the disk usage for your MySQL databases here.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/disk-space-summary.gif" 
        alt="Disk Space Usage summary in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Check Bandwidth Usage

Follow these steps to monitor your website's bandwidth consumption.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Bandwidth">
    In the **Metrics** section of cPanel, click on the **Bandwidth** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/bandwidth-usage-icon.gif"

        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
