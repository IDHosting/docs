---
title: "Change the Language of Your cPanel Account"
description: "Learn how to easily change the display language of your cPanel account to your preferred language."
---

cPanel supports a wide variety of languages. If you wish to change your cPanel interface to a language you are more comfortable with, follow the steps below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel account by navigating to your cPanel URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Change Language">
    Once logged in, locate the **Preferences** section on the left-hand sidebar. Within this section, click on the **Change Language** icon.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/change-language-icon.gif"
        alt="cPanel Change Language Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Confirm Language">
    A new page will load displaying a drop-down menu. Select your desired language from this menu. After making your selection, click the **Change** button to apply the new language.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/change-langauge-config.gif"
        alt="cPanel Language Configuration"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your cPanel interface language has now been successfully updated.
