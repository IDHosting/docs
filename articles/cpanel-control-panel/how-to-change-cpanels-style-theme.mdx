---
title: "Change cPanel's Style/Theme"
description: "Learn how to easily switch between different themes and styles in your cPanel account."
---

Switching to a different cPanel theme is a straightforward process. This guide will walk you through the steps to change your cPanel's appearance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Access your cPanel account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate Theme Option">
    Once logged in, navigate to the **General Information** panel. You will find a **Theme** option with a dropdown menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cpanel-change-theme.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Theme selection dropdown in General Information panel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Apply New Theme">
    Choose your preferred theme from the dropdown list. The selected theme will be applied automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully changed the theme in your cPanel account.
