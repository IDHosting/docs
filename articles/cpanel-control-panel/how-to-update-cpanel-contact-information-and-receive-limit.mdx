---
title: "Update cPanel Contact Information and Receive Resource Limit Notifications"
description: "Learn how to update your contact information in cPanel and set up notifications for resource limit usage."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel account using your provided credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Contact Information">
    In the **Preferences** section of your cPanel dashboard, click on **Contact Information**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/contact-password-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Contact Information Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Email Addresses">
    Enter or update your **primary email address** to receive important account notifications and password reset confirmations. You can also add a secondary email address for redundancy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Enter your cPanel password to verify your identity and then click the **Save** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/contact-info-settings.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Contact Information Settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your email address has now been successfully updated. You will receive notifications regarding your account and resource usage at the email addresses provided.

<Info>
  Ensure your primary email address is accurate to avoid missing critical notifications about your hosting account, including resource limit warnings and password reset links.
</Info>
