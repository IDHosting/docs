---
title: "Update a Cronjob Email Address in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to update the email address associated with your cPanel cronjobs for notifications."
---

You can enable cronjob notifications or update a cronjob's email address in cPanel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Access your cPanel account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Cron Jobs">
    In the **Advanced** section of your cPanel dashboard, click on **Cron Jobs**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cronjob-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Cron Jobs icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Email Address">
    Under the **Cron Email** section, enter your desired email address in the provided text field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Update Email** button to save your changes.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cronjob-email.gif" 
        alt="Update Email button in cPanel Cron Jobs"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
