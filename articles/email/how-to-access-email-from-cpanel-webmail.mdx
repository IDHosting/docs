---
title: "Access Email from cPanel Webmail"
description: "Learn how to access your email account directly through cPanel Webmail."
---

You can access your email account directly from Webmail. Follow these steps to log in and use the webmail client.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Webmail">
    Open your web browser and go to `http://yourdomain.com/webmail`. Replace `yourdomain.com` with your actual domain name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Enter your full email address and password in the provided fields. Click on the **Login** button.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/webmail-direct-login.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Webmail direct login screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Webmail Client">
    Once logged in, you will see the cPanel Webmail page. Click on any of the available webmail email readers, such as **Roundcube** or any other listed application.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/webmail-options.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Webmail client selection options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Email">
    You can now use the selected Webmail Email Reader to read, compose, and manage your emails.
  </Step>
</Steps>
