---
title: "Set Up a cPanel Email Account With Outlook 2021"
description: "Learn how to configure your cPanel email account in Microsoft Outlook 2021 for seamless email management."
---

You can set up a cPanel email account with Microsoft Outlook 2021 and manage your email account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outlook 2021">
    Launch the Outlook 2021 application.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-icon.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Account">
    Navigate to **File** > **Info** and click on **Add Account**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-add-account.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 Add Account screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email Address">
    Enter your full email address (e.g., `yourname@example.com`) and click **Connect**.
    <Note>If you have not selected "Let me set up my account manually," you can skip the next two steps.</Note>

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-add-new-email.gif" 
        alt="Outlook Add New Email screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose IMAP">
    In the **Advanced Setup** options, select **IMAP**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-advance-setup-choose-imap.gif" 
        alt="Outlook Advanced Setup choose IMAP"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter IMAP Details">
    Enter your IMAP details. Ensure you use your server hostname and the correct IMAP port.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-imap-setting.gif" 
        alt="Outlook IMAP settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Password">
    Enter your email password and click **Connect**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-enter-password.gif" 
        alt="Outlook Enter Password screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Setup">
    Finally, click **Done** to complete the setup.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-imap-final.gif" 
        alt="Outlook IMAP final confirmation"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can now manage your newly added email from the left sidebar under the **Home** tab of Outlook.

<Frame>
  <img 
    src="/images/kb/outlook-mail-left-sidebar-access.gif" 
    alt="Outlook mail left sidebar access"
    style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
  />
</Frame>

Your cPanel email is now ready to use with Outlook 2021.
