---
title: "Check for New Email in Outlook 2021"
description: "Learn how to check for new email messages in Outlook 2021 with this step-by-step guide."
---

This guide will walk you through the process of checking for new email messages in Outlook 2021.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outlook 2021">
    First, locate and open the Outlook 2021 application.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-icon.gif"
        alt="Outlook 2021 application icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expand Your Email Account">
    In the left-hand panel of Outlook, you will see a list of your email accounts. Double-click on the account you wish to check to expand it.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/outlook-click-inbox-to-sync.gif"
        alt="Outlook left panel showing email accounts"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Inbox">
    Click on the **Inbox** folder for the selected account. Outlook will begin syncing, and any new emails will appear.

    <Note>
      Clicking on the Inbox initiates the synchronization process, ensuring you see the latest messages.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You are now ready to easily check for new emails in Outlook 2021.
