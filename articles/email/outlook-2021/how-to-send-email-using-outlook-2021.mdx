---
title: "Send Email Using Outlook 2021"
description: "Learn how to send emails directly from Outlook 2021 without needing to access cPanel webmail."
---

You can send emails using Outlook 2021 directly, without needing to manually log in to cPanel webmail. This guide will walk you through the process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outlook 2021">
    Launch the Outlook 2021 application on your computer.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-icon.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Email">
    In the **Home** tab, click on the **New Email** button.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-new-email-icon.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 New Email button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose Your Email">
    A new email window will appear. Fill in the following details:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>From:</strong> If you have multiple email accounts configured, click on **From** and select the desired email account to send from.</li>
      <li><strong>To:</strong> Enter the email address of the recipient.</li>
      <li><strong>Subject:</strong> Enter a concise subject for your email.</li>
    </ul>

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-send-email.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 compose email window"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>

    Write the body of your email in the message box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Your Email">
    Once you have finished composing your email, click the **Send** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully learned how to send emails using Outlook 2021.
