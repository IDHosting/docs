---
title: "Forward Email in Outlook 2021"
description: "Learn how to forward emails using Outlook 2021 with this step-by-step guide."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outlook 2021">
    Launch the Outlook 2021 application.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-icon.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Forward Email">
    Navigate to your **Inbox**. Right-click on the message you wish to forward and select **Forward**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-forward-icon.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 Forward option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose and Send">
    In the new email window, enter the recipient's email address in the **To** field. Compose your message in the body of the email. Once ready, click **Send**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-forward-email.gif" 
        alt="Composing and sending a forwarded email in Outlook"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  This guide covers the basic steps for forwarding an email in Outlook 2021.
</Info>
