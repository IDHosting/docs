---
title: "Reply to an Email in Outlook 2021"
description: "Learn how to reply to emails directly within Outlook 2021."
---

You can reply to emails using Outlook 2021 instead of cPanel Webmail.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outlook 2021">
    Locate and open the Outlook 2021 application on your computer.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-2021-icon.gif" 
        alt="Outlook 2021 application icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Email and Reply">
    In your Outlook inbox, **right-click** on the email you wish to reply to. From the context menu, select the **Reply** option.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-reply-email.gif" 
        alt="Right-clicking an email and selecting Reply in Outlook"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose and Send Your Reply">
    A response box will appear on the right side of your Outlook window. Type your message in this box. Once you are finished composing your reply, click the **Send** button.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/outlook-reply-final.gif" 
        alt="Composing and sending an email reply in Outlook"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Now you know how to easily reply to emails in Outlook 2021.
