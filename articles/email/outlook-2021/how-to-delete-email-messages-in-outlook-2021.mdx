---
title: "Delete Email Messages in Outlook 2021"
description: "Learn how to easily delete email messages in Outlook 2021."
---

You can easily remove email messages in Outlook 2021. The behavior of deletion depends on your email protocol:

*   **POP Protocol:** Emails are deleted only from Outlook 2021 and remain on the mail server.
*   **IMAP Protocol:** Emails are deleted from both Outlook 2021 and the mail server.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outlook 2021">
    Locate and open the Outlook 2021 application.

  <Step title="Delete the Message">
    Navigate to your **Inbox**. Right-click on the email message you wish to delete, and then select **Delete** from the context menu.
