---
title: "Forward an Email in Thunderbird"
description: "Learn how to forward emails to other accounts using the Thunderbird email client."
---

You can easily forward an email to other email accounts using Thunderbird. Follow these steps to do so.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Thunderbird">
    Launch the Thunderbird application on your computer.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-cpanel-icon.gif" 
        alt="Mozilla Thunderbird icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Forward Message">
    Navigate to your **Inbox**. Select the email message you wish to forward. The **Forward** option will appear. Click on it.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/forward-email-select-message.gif" 
        alt="Selecting a message to forward in Thunderbird"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose and Send">
    A new compose window will open with the original message included. Write your message in the body, enter the recipient's email address in the **To** field, and then click **Send**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-email-forwarded.gif" 
        alt="Composing and sending a forwarded email in Thunderbird"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully learned how to forward emails using Thunderbird.
