---
title: "Check for New Email in Thunderbird"
description: "Learn how to check for new emails in the Thunderbird email client."
---

<Info>
  Thunderbird is an email client software. You can work with it in the following way.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Thunderbird">
    Launch the Thunderbird application.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-cpanel-icon.gif"
        alt="Mozilla Thunderbird cPanel Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate Your Inbox">
    In the left panel, you will see a list of your email accounts. Click on **Inbox**. If it is hidden, click on your email address to expand it and reveal the inbox, trash list, and other folders.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/check-email-in-thunderbird.gif"
        alt="Check Email in Thunderbird Interface"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    Alternatively, you can right-click on your email address and select **Get Messages**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Thunderbird automatically checks for new emails every 5 to 10 minutes. It also performs an automatic check whenever you open the program.
</Note>
