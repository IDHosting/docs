---
title: "Reply to Email in Mozilla Thunderbird"
description: "Learn how to reply to emails using Mozilla Thunderbird."
---

You can reply to an email using Thunderbird instead of the cPanel Webmail.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Thunderbird">
    Launch the Mozilla Thunderbird application.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-cpanel-icon.gif"
        alt="Mozilla Thunderbird icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Email">
    Double-click on the email you wish to reply to.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/open-email.gif"
        alt="Opening an email in Thunderbird"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Reply">
    In the opened email view, click the **Reply** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/reply-to-email.gif"
        alt="Reply button in Thunderbird"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose and Send">
    Type your message in the provided text box and then click the **Send** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/send-reply-mozilla-thunderbird.gif"
        alt="Composing and sending a reply in Thunderbird"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
