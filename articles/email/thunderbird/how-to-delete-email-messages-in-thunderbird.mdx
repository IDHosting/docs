---
title: "Delete Email Messages in Thunderbird"
description: "Learn how to easily delete email messages in the Thunderbird email client."
---

You can easily remove emails in Thunderbird. The behavior of deletion depends on the email protocol you are using:

*   **POP:** Emails are deleted only from Thunderbird and remain on the mail server.
*   **IMAP:** Emails are deleted from both Thunderbird and the mail server.

## Steps to Delete an Email

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Thunderbird">
    Launch the Thunderbird application on your computer.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-cpanel-icon.gif"
        alt="Mozilla Thunderbird icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate and Delete the Email">
    Navigate to your **Inbox** (or any other folder containing the email).
    **Right-click** on the email message you wish to delete.
    From the context menu, select **Delete Message**.

      <img
        src="/images/kb/thunderbird-delete-email.gif"

  </Step>
</Steps>
