Thunderbird is a user-friendly email client that allows you to manage your emails efficiently. This guide will walk you through the steps to set up your cPanel email account in Thunderbird, enabling you to send and receive emails seamlessly.

## Setting Up Your cPanel Email in Thunderbird

Follow these steps to add your cPanel email account to Thunderbird:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Thunderbird">
    Launch the Thunderbird application on your computer.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-cpanel-icon.gif"
        alt="Thunderbird application icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to New Account">
    Click on the **menu icon** (â˜°) located on the right side of the screen. From the drop-down menu, select **New Account**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-menu.gif"
        alt="Thunderbird menu with New Account option highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Existing Email">
    In the new options that appear, click on **Existing Email**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/thunderbird-existing-mail-account.gif"
        alt="Thunderbird new account setup screen with Existing Email option highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Account Details">
    In the **Mail Setup** window, fill in your **Your name**, your **Email address** (your full cPanel email address), and your **Password**. Then, click **Continue**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/thunderbird-mail-setup-account-cpanel.gif"
        alt="Thunderbird Mail Setup Account screen for cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Setup">
    Thunderbird will attempt to automatically configure your email account settings. This may take a few moments. Once completed, click **Done**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/thunderbird-email-setup-done.gif"
        alt="Thunderbird email setup completion screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Security Exception (If prompted)">
    If you encounter an **SSL Security Exception** warning, click on **Confirm Security Exception** to proceed. This warning will then disappear.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-confirm-security-exception.gif"
        alt="Thunderbird SSL Security Exception confirmation dialog"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Congratulations! Your cPanel email account is now successfully set up in Thunderbird. You can begin managing your emails directly through the application.
