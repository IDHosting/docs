---
title: "Send an Email Using Thunderbird"
description: "Learn how to send emails directly from the Thunderbird email client without needing to access cPanel webmail."
---

<Info>
You can send emails using Thunderbird without logging into cPanel webmail manually. This guide will walk you through the process.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Thunderbird">
    Launch the Thunderbird application on your computer.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/mozilla-thunderbird-cpanel-icon.gif" 
        alt="Mozilla Thunderbird icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose a New Message">
    In the top menu, click on the **New Message** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/send-email-using-thunderbird.gif" 
        alt="Thunderbird New Message button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Email Details">
    <p>If you have multiple email accounts configured in Thunderbird, click on your account name to reveal a dropdown menu. Select the appropriate email account you wish to send from.</p>
    <p>Enter the recipient's email address in the **To** field.</p>
    <p>In the **Subject** field, type the subject of your email.</p>
    <p>Compose your message in the main message box.</p>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/thunderbird-compose-email.gif" 
        alt="Thunderbird compose email window"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the Email">
    Once you have finished composing your email, click the **Send** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<p>We hope you found this guide helpful!</p>
