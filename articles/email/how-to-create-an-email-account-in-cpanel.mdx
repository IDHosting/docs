---
title: "Create an Email Account in cPanel"
description: "A step-by-step guide on how to create a new email account using cPanel."
---

Follow these steps to create a new email address in your cPanel account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Begin by logging into your cPanel account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Accounts">
    In the **Email** section, click on **Email Accounts**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/email-account-icon.gif"
        alt="cPanel Email Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Creation">
    Click on the **+Create** button, typically located on the right-hand side of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Account Details">
    Under the **Create An Email Account** section, you will need to fill in the following details:
    *   **Domain:** If you have multiple domains associated with your account, select the desired domain from the dropdown list.
    *   **Username:** Enter the desired username for your email address (e.g., your first name).
    *   **Password:** Choose **Set password now**, enter a secure password, or use the password generator to create one.
    *   **Optional Settings:** Click on **Edit Settings** to expand this section. In the **Storage space** option, specify the amount of storage space for this mailbox or select the **Unlimited** option.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/email-account-creation.gif"
        alt="cPanel Email Account Creation Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Creation">
    Once all the details are entered, click on the **Create** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After your email account is successfully created, it will be listed under the **Account & Domain** tab.
