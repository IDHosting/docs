---
title: "Update the DNS Nameservers at 123-Reg"
description: "This guide explains how to update your domain's nameservers to our nameservers if your domain is registered with 123-Reg."
---

You can find our nameservers in your hosting welcome email. This tutorial will guide you through updating your domain's nameservers if your domain is registered on **123-Reg**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to 123-Reg">
    Open the 123-Reg website and click on **Sign In**. Then, click on "<strong>Sign-in</strong>" in the pop-up window.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/controlpanel-123-reg.gif" 
        alt="123-Reg control panel sign-in"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter the username and password of your 123-reg account and click on **Sign-in**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/login-123-reg.gif" 
        alt="123-Reg login page"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to DNS management">
    Under the **Domains section**, click on **DNS** next to the domain you wish to manage.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/123-reg-manage.gif" 
        alt="123-Reg manage domain DNS"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Nameservers">
    Click on the **Nameservers** tab and then on **Change Nameservers**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/domain-select-123-reg.gif" 
        alt="123-Reg domain nameserver selection"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your nameservers">
    Choose the "<strong>I'll use my own nameservers</strong>" option, and enter your nameservers in the designated fields.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/edit-ns-123-reg.gif" 
        alt="123-Reg edit nameservers"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    You should see a success message upon completion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  It may take anywhere from 12 to 24 hours for the changes to take effect worldwide.
</Note>
