---
title: "Update the DNS Nameservers at Namecheap"
description: "A guide on how to update your domain's nameservers to our nameservers if your domain is registered with Namecheap."
---

You can find our nameservers in your hosting welcome email. This tutorial will guide you through updating your domain's nameservers if your domain is registered on **Namecheap**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In to Namecheap">
    Open the Namecheap.com website and click on **Sign In**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/namecheap-login-page.gif" 
        alt="Namecheap login page"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Domain List">
    From the left-side menu, click on **Domain List**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/domain-list-menu-namecheap.gif" 
        alt="Namecheap domain list menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Domain">
    Under **Domain List**, locate your domain and click **Manage** next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/login-namecheap.gif" 
        alt="Namecheap manage domain"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Custom DNS">
    Scroll down to the **Nameservers** option and select **Custom DNS** from the drop-down menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/domain-edit-namecheap.gif" 
        alt="Namecheap custom DNS selection"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Nameservers and Save">
    Enter your nameservers in the provided text fields and click on the **Save** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/namecheap-transfer-dns.gif" 
        alt="Namecheap enter nameservers"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  It may take anywhere from 12 to 24 hours for the DNS changes to take effect worldwide.
</Note>
