---
title: "Update DNS Nameservers at NetEarthOne or LogicBoxes-Based Registrars"
description: "A guide on how to update your domain's nameservers if your domain is registered with NetEarthOne or a LogicBoxes-based registrar."
---

<Note>You can find our nameservers in your hosting welcome email. You need to update your domain nameservers to our nameservers.</Note>

If your domain is registered on NetEarthOne or LogicBoxes-based registrars, follow this tutorial to update your domain's Nameservers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your registrar account">
    Open the NetEarthOne.com or LogicBoxes-based registrar website and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Order Management">
    After a successful login, click on **List/Search Orders** from the **Manage Orders** drop-down menu.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/neo-list-domain.gif"
        alt="NetEarthOne - List/Search Orders"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your domain">
    Under **Domain Name**, click on the appropriate domain you wish to update.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/select-logicboxes-domain.gif"
        alt="LogicBoxes - Select Domain"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Nameserver settings">
    Scroll down to the **Domain Registration** option and click on **Name Servers**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/update-ns-neo.gif"
        alt="NetEarthOne - Update Nameservers"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Nameservers">
    Enter the nameservers found in your Welcome Email and click on **Update Name Servers**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/dns-update-neo.gif"
        alt="NetEarthOne - Enter New Nameservers"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>It may take anywhere from 12 to 24 hours for the DNS changes to propagate worldwide.</Note>
