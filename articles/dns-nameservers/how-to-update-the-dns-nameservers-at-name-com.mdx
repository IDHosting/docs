---
title: "Update the DNS Nameservers at Name.com"
description: "A guide on how to update your domain's nameservers to our nameservers when your domain is registered with Name.com."
---

You can find our nameservers in your hosting welcome email. You need to update your domain nameservers to our nameservers.

If your domain is registered on **Name.com**, follow this tutorial to update your domain's Nameservers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Name.com">
    Open the Name.com website and click on **Log in**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/name-com-login-link.gif" 
        alt="Name.com login link"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Enter the username and password of your account and then click on **Log in**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/login-page.gif" 
        alt="Name.com login page with username and password fields"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to My Domains">
    After a successful login, click on **My Domains**. You will see all of your active domain names under the **Domain Name** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Domain">
    Click on the domain for which you want to update the nameservers.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/your-domain-click.gif" 
        alt="List of domains with one selected"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Nameservers">
    Scroll down to the Nameservers section and click **Manage Nameservers**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/nameserver-namecom.gif" 
        alt="Name.com domain management page with Nameservers section highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Nameservers">
    Navigate to the **Add Nameserver** section. Enter our nameserver in the text field (e.g., `ns1.example.com`). Then click on **Add Nameserver**. Repeat this process to add our second nameserver.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/adding-nameserver-namecom.gif" 
        alt="Name.com interface for adding nameservers"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **Save Changes**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  You need to check your Hosting Welcome E-mail for your current nameservers. In case you have lost it, contact us, and we will be glad to assist you. It may take anywhere from 12 to 24 hours for the changes to take effect worldwide.
</Note>
