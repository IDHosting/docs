---
title: "Update the DNS Nameservers at GoDaddy"
description: "A guide on how to update your domain's nameservers to our nameservers when your domain is registered at GoDaddy."
---

You can find our nameservers in your hosting welcome email. This tutorial will guide you through updating your domain's nameservers if your domain is registered at **GoDaddy**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In to GoDaddy">
    Navigate to the GoDaddy website and click on **Sign In**. Under the "Registered Users" section, click **Sign-in** again to be redirected to the login page.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/godaddy-log-in-form.gif"
        alt="GoDaddy login form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Enter your GoDaddy **Username or Customer ID** and **password**, then click **Sign In**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/login-godaddy.gif"
        alt="GoDaddy login with username and password"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to DNS Management">
    Under the **Domains** section, click on **DNS** next to the domain you wish to manage.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/godaddy-manage.gif"
        alt="GoDaddy domain management page"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Nameservers">
    Click on the **Nameservers** tab, and then click on **Change Nameservers**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/domain-select-godaddy.gif"
        alt="GoDaddy change nameservers option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Nameservers">
    Select the option **"I'll use my own nameservers"** and enter our nameservers in the provided fields.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/edit-ns-godaddy.gif"
        alt="GoDaddy interface for editing nameservers"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click on **Save** to confirm the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Please be aware that it may take between 12 to 24 hours for these DNS changes to propagate globally.
</Note>
