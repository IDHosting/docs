---
title: "Update the DNS Nameservers at DynaDot"
description: "This tutorial will guide you through the process of updating your domain's nameservers to our nameservers if your domain is registered with DynaDot."
---

You can find our nameservers in your hosting welcome email. You need to update your domain nameservers to our nameservers. If your domain is registered on **DynaDot**, follow this tutorial to update your domain's Nameservers.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your DynaDot Account">
    Open the DynaDot website and click on **Login**. It will redirect you to a login page.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/login-dynadot.gif"
        alt="DynaDot login page"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    Enter your username and password, then click on **Sign-in**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Domain Management">
    Click on **My Domains** from the left-side menu, then select **Manage Domains** in the drop-down.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/dynadot-manage.gif"
        alt="DynaDot My Domains menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select DNS Settings">
    Check the box next to your domain name(s) and click on **DNS Settings** from the **Action** menu.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/domain-select-dynadot.gif"
        alt="DynaDot domain selection for DNS settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Name Servers Option">
    Select the **Name Servers** option from the drop-down menu.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/ns-setting-dynadot.gif"
        alt="DynaDot DNS settings with Name Servers option selected"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update and Save Nameservers">
    Enter your nameservers in the provided text boxes and click **Save Name Server** to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Check your Hosting Welcome Email to find your current nameservers. If you cannot find it, please do not hesitate to contact us for assistance.

<Note>
  DNS propagation can take 12-24 hours to take effect globally.
</Note>
