This guide will walk you through setting up or disabling automatic email synchronization for your cPanel email account on your Android device. This ensures you receive new email notifications promptly or helps conserve data if you prefer manual syncing.

## Configure Email Sync Frequency

Follow these steps to adjust your email synchronization settings:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Email App">
    Open the **Inbox** or **Gmail** application on your Android device.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/android-email-icon-cpanel.gif"
        alt="Android cPanel Email Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    On the top left, tap on the **Menu** icon (usually three horizontal lines). Scroll down to the bottom of the menu and select **Settings**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/android-email-setting-change.gif"
        alt="Android Email Settings Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Email Account">
    Choose the cPanel email account you wish to configure from the list of accounts.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/select-email-android-email-cpanel.gif"
        alt="Select cPanel Email Account on Android"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust Sync Frequency">
    Tap on **Sync frequency**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/set-sync-android-cpanel-email-config.gif"
        alt="Set Sync Frequency for cPanel Email on Android"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Sync Interval">
    Choose your desired synchronization frequency. You can select **15 minutes** or any other custom time interval.
    <Tip>
      If you prefer to sync emails manually and avoid automatic background syncing, select **Never**. In this case, you will need to manually refresh your inbox to receive new emails.
    </Tip>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sync-cpanel-android-mobile-config.gif"
        alt="cPanel Email Sync Configuration on Android"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

By adjusting the sync frequency, you gain better control over how your cPanel email is updated on your Android device, optimizing both your notification experience and data usage.
