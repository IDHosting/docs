---
title: "Read cPanel Emails in Android"
description: "Learn how to add a Gmail widget to your Android home screen for quick access to your cPanel emails."
---

If you've already added your cPanel email accounts to your Android's built-in email application, you can enhance convenience by adding a widget to your home screen. This allows you to easily access and read your cPanel emails with a single tap.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Widgets">
    On your Android device's home screen, tap and hold an empty area. Several options will appear, including **Wallpaper** and **Widgets**. Tap on **Widgets**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/read-cpanel-email-on-android.gif" 
        alt="Android home screen showing widget options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Gmail Widget">
    Scroll through the available widgets until you find **Gmail**. Tap and hold the **Gmail widget**, then drag it to your desired location on your Android home screen and release.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/android-built-in-widget-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="Dragging the Gmail widget to the Android home screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/android-drag-built-in-icon.gif" 
        alt="Gmail widget placed on the Android home screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Email Account and Folder">
    If you have multiple email accounts configured, you'll be prompted to select the appropriate cPanel email account. After selecting the account, choose the **Inbox** folder.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/android-inbox-cpanel-setup-email.gif" 
        alt="Selecting the Inbox folder for the cPanel email account"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Setup">
    Tap **Done** to finalize the widget setup.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/android-inbox-create-android-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="Confirming the widget setup"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Emails">
    To open the widget and view your emails, tap on **Take Me to Gmail**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Congratulations! You can now easily read, compose, and manage your cPanel emails directly from the icon on your home screen. The widget will automatically sync new emails when opened, and notifications will appear on your screen for incoming messages.
