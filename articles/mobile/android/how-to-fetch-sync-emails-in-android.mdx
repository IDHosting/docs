---
title: "Fetch - Sync Emails in Android"
description: "Learn how to manually sync your emails on your Android device using the built-in email app."
---

This guide will walk you through the process of manually fetching new emails on your Android device. Ensure you have already set up your cPanel email account in your Android's built-in email application.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Inbox">
    Open your email application and tap on the **Inbox** folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Sync">
    From the top of the screen, pull down. You will see a refresh icon appear and start spinning, indicating that the app is syncing your emails.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/android-swipe-down-refreshemail-cpanel.gif"
        alt="Pulling down on the Android email inbox to refresh and sync emails."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    Once the sync is complete, any new emails will appear in your inbox.
  </Step>
</Steps>
