---
title: "Set Up an Email Signature in Android"
description: "Learn how to set a custom email signature in the built-in Android email application."
---

The built-in Android email application allows you to set a custom email signature that will be automatically appended to the end of your outgoing emails. This guide will walk you through the process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Email App">
    Open your **Inbox** or **Gmail** application on your Android device.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/android-email-icon-cpanel.gif"
        alt="Android Email App Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Tap on the **Menu** icon in the top left corner. Scroll down to the bottom of the menu and tap on **Settings**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/android-email-setting-change.gif"
        alt="Accessing Email Settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Email Account">
    Choose the email account for which you want to set a signature. If you have multiple accounts configured, you can set a unique signature for each.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/select-email-android-email-cpanel.gif"
        alt="Selecting Email Account for Signature"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Signature Settings">
    Tap on the **Mobile Signature** option.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/set-email-signature-android-cpanel.gif"
        alt="Tapping Mobile Signature Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize and Confirm">
    Enter your desired customized signature in the provided field. Once you are satisfied, tap **OK** to save your changes.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/signature-customize-cpanel-android.gif"
        alt="Customizing Email Signature"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your email signature will now be automatically added to the end of all emails you send or reply to from your Android device.
