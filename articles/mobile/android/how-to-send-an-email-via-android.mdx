This guide will walk you through the steps to send an email from your Android device using the built-in email application, assuming you have already set up your cPanel email account.

<Note>This tutorial assumes you have already completed the setup of your cPanel email account on your Android device and have the email widget added to your home screen.</Note>

## Sending an Email

Follow these steps to compose and send an email:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Email Application">
    Locate and open your **Email** inbox or **Gmail** application on your Android device.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/android-email-icon-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="Android Email Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose a New Email">
    In the Mail app, tap on the **Compose** button to start a new email.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/android-cpanel-compose-send-email.gif" 
        alt="Android cPanel Compose Email"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Email Details and Send">
    1. Enter the recipient's email address in the **To** field.
    2. Add a **Subject** for your email.
    3. Type your message in the main body of the email.
    4. Once you are ready, tap on the **Send** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/email-send-to-android-cpanel-tutorial.gif" 
        alt="Email Send To Android cPanel Tutorial"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully learned how to send an email via your Android device using your cPanel email account.
