---
title: "Delete cPanel Emails in Android"
description: "A guide on how to delete emails from your cPanel account using the built-in email application on an Android device."
---

This guide will walk you through the process of deleting emails from your cPanel account on your Android device.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Email Inbox">
    Tap on the **Email inbox** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/android-email-icon-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="Android cPanel email inbox icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Email for Deletion">
    Tap and hold on the email you wish to delete for a few seconds. This will select the email.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/email-select-android-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="Selecting an email for deletion in Android"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Email">
    Tap the **Delete** icon that appears at the top of the screen.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/delete-email-android-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="Delete icon for emails on Android"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You will see a confirmation message indicating that the email has been deleted.

<Frame>
  <img 
    src="/images/kb/android-email-deleted-cpanel.gif" 
    alt="Email deleted confirmation on Android"
    style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
  />
</Frame>

<Info>
  If you wish to restore the deleted email, tap on **Undo** shortly after deletion. This option will disappear after a short period.
</Info>

You have now successfully deleted cPanel emails from your Android device.
