---
title: "Set Up a cPanel Email Account in Android"
description: "Learn how to add your cPanel email account to your Android device's built-in email application."
---

You can add a cPanel email account to your Android's built-in email application without needing any external apps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    On your Android device, swipe up from the bottom of the screen and find the **Settings** icon. Tap to open it.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/open-settings-android-mobile.gif" alt="Open Settings on Android Mobile" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Accounts">
    Scroll down and tap on **Passwords, passkeys & accounts**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/add-account-android-email.gif" alt="Add Account in Android Email Settings" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Account">
    Tap on **+ Add account**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/add-email-android-plus.gif" alt="Add Email Account Plus Button" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Personal IMAP">
    Choose the **Personal IMAP** option.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/android-imap-cpanel-add.gif" alt="Android IMAP cPanel Add Account" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email and Password">
    Enter your full email address (e.g., `you@example.com`) and tap **Next**. Then, enter your password and tap **Next** again.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/email-setup-android.gif" alt="Email Setup on Android" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Server Settings">
    Review the **Incoming Server Settings** and **Outgoing Mail Server Settings**. If they look correct, tap **Next**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/android-incoming-outgoing-mail.gif" alt="Android Incoming and Outgoing Mail Server Settings" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Sync Frequency">
    Select your preferred **Email Sync Frequency** and other options under the accounts settings. Press **Next** when done.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/final-setup-email-android.gif" alt="Final Email Setup on Android" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Name">
    In the **Your Name** input box, enter the name you want displayed on outgoing emails, and then press **Next**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Congratulations! You will now receive notifications for new emails. To view your email accounts, navigate to **Settings** > **Accounts and Backup** > **Manage accounts** on your Android device.

You can also access your email directly from the built-in Gmail application.
