---
title: "Add a cPanel Email Account on Apple/iOS"
description: "Learn how to add your cPanel email account to your Apple iOS device."
---

You can add a cPanel email account to your Apple iOS mobile device to automatically check emails at set intervals or manually. You'll also receive new email notifications on your phone.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    On your iPhone or iPad, open the main screen and tap on the **Settings** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ios-apple-home-screen.gif" 
        alt="iOS Apple Home Screen with Settings icon highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Accounts">
    Scroll down and tap on **Passwords & Accounts**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ios-apple-passwords-and-accounts.gif" 
        alt="iOS Passwords & Accounts screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Account">
    Tap on **Add Account**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ios-add-mail-account.gif" 
        alt="iOS Add Account screen with Mail option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Other">
    Choose the **Other** option.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/apple-other-email-option.gif" 
        alt="iOS email account type selection with Other highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Mail Account">
    Tap on **Add Mail Account**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ios-add-mail-account-option.gif" 
        alt="iOS Add Mail Account screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email Details">
    Enter the following details:
    *   **Email:** Your full cPanel email address (e.g., `user@example.com`).
    *   **Description:** A name for the account (e.g., "Support Account").

    **Incoming Mail Server:**
    *   **Hostname:** Your domain name (e.g., `example.com`).
    *   **Username:** Your full cPanel email address.
    *   **Password:** Your email account password.

    **Outgoing Mail Server:**
    *   **Hostname:** Your domain name (e.g., `example.com`).
    *   **Username:** Your full cPanel email address.
    *   **Password:** Your email account password.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/email-apple-email-ac-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="iOS email account setup screen with cPanel details"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    After filling in the details, tap **Next**. It may take a few minutes to verify the information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Email">
    Once the email account has been added successfully, you can access it from the **Apple Mail App**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ios-mail-icon-home.gif" 
        alt="iOS Home Screen with Mail app icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
