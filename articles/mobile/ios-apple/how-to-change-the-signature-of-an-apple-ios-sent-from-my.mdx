---
title: "Change the Signature of an Apple/iOS Device"
description: "Learn how to customize the default 'Sent from my iPhone' signature on your Apple devices."
---

When you send an email from an iOS device, a default signature like "Sent from my iPhone" is automatically added. This guide will show you how to change this default signature to something personalized.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    On your iPhone or iPad's **main screen**, tap on the **Settings** icon.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ios-apple-home-screen.gif" 
        alt="iOS Apple Home Screen with Settings icon highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mail Settings">
    Scroll down within the Settings app and tap on **Mail**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ios-settings-mail-option.gif" 
        alt="iOS Settings screen with Mail option highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Signature Settings">
    In the Mail settings, tap on **Signature**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/set-signature-apple-email-cpanel.gif" 
        alt="Mail settings screen with Signature option highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Signature">
    You can now customize your email signature. Delete the default text and type in your desired signature.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/customize-apple-ios-signature.gif" 
        alt="iOS signature customization screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once you have changed and saved your signature, all outgoing emails from your iOS device will use your customized signature instead of the default one.
