---
title: "Enable or Disable PHP's allow_url_fopen Using CloudLinux Selector in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to enable or disable the allow_url_fopen directive in PHP using the CloudLinux Selector within your Plesk control panel."
---

You can use the CloudLinux Selector to easily enable or disable the `allow_url_fopen` directive in PHP through your Plesk account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Plesk">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to PHP Selector">
    In the right sidebar, click on the **Pull icon** and then select **PHP Selector**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-cl-php-selector-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk CloudLinux PHP Selector Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Options">
    Click on the **Options** menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cl-options-menu.gif" 
        alt="CloudLinux Options Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    <Note>If the **Options** page displays an error related to the native PHP version, you must first change it to a non-native version before proceeding.</Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure allow_url_fopen">
    To enable `allow_url_fopen`, check the corresponding box. To disable it, uncheck the box.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cl-fopen-enable-disable.gif" 
        alt="CloudLinux allow_url_fopen Enable/Disable"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    A confirmation message will appear indicating whether the option has been enabled or disabled.
  </Step>
</Steps>
