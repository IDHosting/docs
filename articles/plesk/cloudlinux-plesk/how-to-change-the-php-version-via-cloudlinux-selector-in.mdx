---
title: "Change the PHP Version via CloudLinux Selector in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to change your website's PHP version using the CloudLinux Selector in Plesk."
---

You can use CloudLinux's Select PHP Version feature to easily change the PHP version for your website directly within your Plesk control panel. CloudLinux offers a variety of PHP versions to choose from, allowing you to select the one that best suits your application's needs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by navigating to your Plesk login URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to PHP Selector">
    In the right sidebar, click on the **Pull icon** and then select **PHP Selector** from the dropdown menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-cl-php-selector-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk CloudLinux PHP Selector menu option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your desired PHP version">
    To the right of the **Current PHP version** label, click on the **drop-down menu**. Choose the PHP version you wish to use for your website from the available options.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-da-cl-change-php-version.gif" 
        alt="Plesk CloudLinux change PHP version dropdown"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the changes">
    After selecting your preferred PHP version, click the **Apply** button to save your changes.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cl-set-as-php-version.gif" 
        alt="CloudLinux set as PHP version button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully changed your website's PHP version using the CloudLinux Selector in Plesk.
