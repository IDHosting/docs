---
title: "Rename an FTP Username in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to rename an existing FTP username in Plesk without needing to delete and recreate the account."
---

You can rename an FTP username in Plesk without deleting or making a new account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    Click on **Websites & Domains** from the Sidebar Menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your Domain">
    Under the list of **Domain names**, click on the appropriate domain name.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-click-on-domain.gif" 
        alt="Plesk dashboard with domain name highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access FTP Settings">
    From the **Dashboard**, under the **Files & Databases** section, click on **FTP**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-ftp-icon.gif" 
        alt="Plesk dashboard FTP icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the FTP Account">
    Click on the FTP account you wish to rename.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-ftp-account-click.gif" 
        alt="Plesk FTP accounts list with one account selected"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename the FTP Account">
    Enter the new FTP name in the **FTP account name** field and click **OK** to confirm.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-ftp-rename-final.gif" 
        alt="Plesk FTP account rename dialog with new name entered"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

The FTP username has now been successfully updated. You can use the new name to access and manage your files as usual.
