---
title: "Change an FTP Account Password in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to change the password for a specific FTP account within your Plesk control panel."
---

To change the password for a specific FTP account in Plesk, follow these steps. Note that changing the main root FTP account password requires changing your Plesk account password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Plesk">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    From the sidebar menu, click on **Websites & Domains**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Domain">
    Under the list of **Domain names**, click on the domain associated with the FTP account you wish to modify.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-click-on-domain.gif" 
        alt="Clicking on a specific domain name in Plesk"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access FTP Settings">
    On the domain's dashboard, locate the **Files & Databases** section and click on **FTP**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-ftp-icon.gif" 
        alt="Plesk FTP icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the FTP Account">
    Click on the specific FTP account for which you want to change the password.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-ftp-account-click.gif" 
        alt="Clicking on a specific FTP account in Plesk"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set New Password">
    In the **Password** field, enter your desired new password. Alternatively, you can click **Generate** to create a strong, random password. Click **Show** to view and copy the generated password.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-ftp-password-change-options.gif" 
        alt="Plesk FTP password change options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Click **OK** to save the new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The FTP account password has now been successfully changed in Plesk. You can use this new password to connect to your server via your FTP client.
