---
title: "Rename an Email Address in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to rename an existing email address in Plesk without deleting the account."
---

In this tutorial, you will learn how to rename an email address in Plesk. This is helpful when you want to update the email name without deleting the account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Plesk">
    Begin by logging in to your Plesk account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mail">
    Once logged in, click on **Mail** from the Sidebar Menu.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-mail-option.gif"
        alt="Plesk Mail Option in Sidebar Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Email Account">
    From the list of email accounts, click on the specific email account you wish to rename.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-email-list.gif"
        alt="Plesk Email List"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename Email Address">
    In the **email address** field, enter the new desired name for your email address.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-rename-email.gif"
        alt="Plesk Rename Email Address Field"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click the **OK** button to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The email address has now been successfully renamed in Plesk. You can use the updated address to send and receive emails.
