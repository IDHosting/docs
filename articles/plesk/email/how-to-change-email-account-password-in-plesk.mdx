---
title: "Change Email Account Password in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to change your email account password within the Plesk control panel."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mail">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Mail**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-mail-option.gif"
        alt="Plesk Mail Option in Sidebar Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Email Account">
    In the **email** list, click on the specific email account for which you want to change the password.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-email-list.gif"
        alt="Plesk Email List"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change the Password">
    In the **Password** field, you can either enter a new password manually or click **Generate** to create a strong, random password.
    <Tip>Click **Show** to reveal the generated password, and then copy it for your records.</Tip>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-change-password-final.gif"
        alt="Plesk Change Password Field"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    Click **OK** to save the new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The email account password has now been successfully changed in Plesk. You can use this new password to access your email.
