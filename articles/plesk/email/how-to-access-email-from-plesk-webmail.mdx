---
title: "Access Email from Plesk Webmail"
description: "Learn how to access your email directly from Plesk Webmail using your domain's webmail interface."
---

You can access your email directly from Plesk Webmail. This guide will walk you through the simple steps to log in and manage your emails.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Webmail">
    Open your web browser and go to the webmail address for your domain. The typical format is `https://webmail.yourdomain.com`. Replace `yourdomain.com` with your actual domain name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Login Credentials">
    On the login page, enter your full email address (e.g., `you@yourdomain.com`) and your email account password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Click on the **Login** button to access your webmail inbox.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-webmail-login-page.gif"
        alt="Plesk Webmail login page with fields for email address and password."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Emails">
    After a successful login, you will be able to view, send, and manage your emails through the webmail interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you have forgotten your email password, you will need to reset it through your Plesk control panel or contact your hosting provider.
</Info>
