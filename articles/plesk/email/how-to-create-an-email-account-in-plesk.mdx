---
title: "Create an Email Account in Plesk"
description: "Follow this tutorial to create an email account in Plesk."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Begin by logging into your Plesk control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mail">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Mail**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-mail-option.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Mail Option in Sidebar Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Email Address">
    Click on the **Create Email Address** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-create-email-address-button.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Create Email Address Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Email Details">
    Under the **General** tab, you will need to fill in the following details:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Email Address:</strong> Enter the desired email address. If you have multiple domains, select the correct one from the dropdown menu.</li>
      <li><strong>Password:</strong> Enter a password manually or click **Generate** to create a strong password. You can click **Show** to view and copy the generated password.</li>
      <li><strong>Mailbox:</strong> Choose between <strong>Default size</strong> or <strong>Another size</strong>, and then specify the storage space for the mailbox.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-create-email-address.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Create Email Address Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Creation">
    Click **OK** to finalize the creation of the email account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The email account has now been successfully created in Plesk. You can begin using your new email address to send and receive emails.
