---
title: "Add a Subdomain in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to add a subdomain in Plesk to create different sections of your website."
---

<Info>
This tutorial will guide you through the process of adding a subdomain in Plesk. Subdomains are useful for organizing different parts of your website, such as a blog or a separate customer portal (e.g., blog.example.com).
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Plesk">
    Begin by logging into your Plesk account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    From the sidebar menu, click on **Websites & Domains**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif"
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Subdomain">
    Click on the **Add Subdomain** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-add-subdomain-button.gif"
        alt="Plesk Add Subdomain button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Subdomain Details">
    Enter your desired **subdomain name** in the provided field. Then, select the main domain from the **Domain** dropdown list.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-add-subdomain-options.gif"
        alt="Plesk Add Subdomain configuration options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Save">
    Click **OK** to create the subdomain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Once your subdomain has been successfully added, you can manage its settings and content from the **Websites & Domains** section in Plesk.
</Note>
