---
title: "Remove a Subdomain in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to remove a subdomain in Plesk by following these simple steps."
---

A subdomain is an additional part of your primary domain. This guide will walk you through the process of removing a subdomain in Plesk.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    From the sidebar menu on the left, click on **Websites & Domains**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the Subdomain">
    Under the list of **Domain names**, locate the subdomain you wish to remove. Click on the **More icon** (three dots) in front of the subdomain and select **Remove subdomain**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-remove-subdomain.gif" 
        alt="Plesk remove subdomain option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Removal">
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Click **Yes** to confirm the removal of the subdomain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The subdomain has now been successfully removed from your Plesk account.
