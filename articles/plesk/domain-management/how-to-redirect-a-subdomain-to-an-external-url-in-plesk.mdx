---
title: "Redirect a Subdomain to an External URL in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to set up a subdomain redirect in Plesk to point it to an external website."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    Click on **Websites & Domains** from the sidebar menu.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif"
        alt="Plesk Websites and Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Hosting Settings">
    Under the list of **Domain names**, locate your subdomain and click on the **Hosting Settings** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-hosting-setting-icon.gif"
        alt="Plesk Hosting Settings icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-open-subdomain.gif"
        alt="Plesk subdomain hosting settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Forwarding">
    Enter the following details:
    <ul>
      <li>
        **Hosting Type**: Select **Forwarding** from the dropdown menu.
      </li>
      <li>
        **Forward visitors to**: Enter the external URL where you want the subdomain to redirect.
      </li>
      <li>
        **Forwarding Type**: Choose the appropriate option (e.g., 301 Permanent or 302 Temporary) based on your needs.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-forwarding-redirect-final.gif"
        alt="Plesk forwarding and redirect settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Save** button. You may see a warning message; confirm it by clicking **Yes**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The subdomain is now successfully redirected to the external URL. Visitors accessing the subdomain will be automatically directed to the specified external website.
