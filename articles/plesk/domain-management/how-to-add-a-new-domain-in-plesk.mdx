---
title: "Add a New Domain in Plesk"
description: "Follow these steps to add a new domain to your Plesk account."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Websites & Domains**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Domain">
    On the **Websites & Domains** page, click the **Add Domain** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-add-domain-button.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Add Domain button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Blank Website">
    Select **Blank Website** as the option for your new domain.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-create-blankwebsite.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Create Blank Website option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Domain Details">
    In the **Adding New Domain** section, provide the following information:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Domain name</strong>: Enter the domain name you wish to add.</li>
      <li><strong>Hosting Type</strong>: Select <strong>Website Hosting</strong> from the dropdown menu. You can leave all other options at their default settings.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-add-domain-options.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Add Domain options form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Domain Addition">
    Click the **Add Domain** button to finalize the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once your domain is successfully added, you can manage it from the **Websites & Domains** section in your Plesk control panel.
