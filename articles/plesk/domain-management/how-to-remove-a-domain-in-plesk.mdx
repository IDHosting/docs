---
title: "Remove a Domain in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to remove a domain from your Plesk account."
---

This article will guide you through the process of removing a domain from your Plesk account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Plesk">
    Begin by logging in to your Plesk account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Websites & Domains**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the Website">
    Under the **list of domains**, locate the domain you wish to remove. Click the **More icon** next to the domain and select **Remove website**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-more-icon.gif" 
        alt="Plesk More icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-remove-domain.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Remove website option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Removal">
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Click **Yes** to confirm the removal of the domain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The domain has now been successfully removed from your Plesk account.
