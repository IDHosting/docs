---
title: "Remove a Subdomain Redirect in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to remove a subdomain redirect in Plesk when you no longer want the subdomain to forward to an external website."
---

In this tutorial, you will learn how to remove a subdomain redirect in Plesk. This is useful when you no longer want the subdomain to forward to an external website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    Click on **Websites & Domains** from the Sidebar Menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Websites and Domains Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Hosting Settings for the Subdomain">
    Under the list of **Domain names**, click on the 
    <img 
      src="/images/kb/plesk-hosting-setting-icon.gif" 
      alt="Plesk Hosting Settings Icon"
      style={{ width: 'auto', height: '1em', verticalAlign: 'middle' }}
    /> 
    **Hosting Settings** icon in the same row as the subdomain you wish to modify.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-open-subdomain.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Open Subdomain Settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Hosting Type">
    Under **Hosting Type**, select the **Website** option from the drop-down menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-remove-forwarding.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Remove Forwarding Settings"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    Click **Next**, then click **Save**. Leave all other options as they are.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The subdomain redirect has now been successfully removed in Plesk.
