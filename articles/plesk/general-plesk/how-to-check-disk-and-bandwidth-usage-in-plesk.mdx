---
title: "Check Disk and Bandwidth Usage in Plesk"
description: "A guide on how to check your disk and bandwidth usage within the Plesk control panel."
---

You can quickly check your disk and bandwidth usage in Plesk by following these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Statistics">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Statistics**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-statistics-menu.gif"
        alt="Plesk Statistics Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Domain">
    If you have multiple domains hosted on your Plesk account, click on the specific domain for which you want to check usage.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-files-multiple-domains.gif"
        alt="Plesk Multiple Domains Selection"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Usage Statistics">
    You will now see a dashboard displaying your resource usage.
    <ul>
      <li>Disk usage is shown on the left side.</li>
      <li>Traffic/bandwidth usage is displayed on the right side.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-disk-and-bandwidth-usage.gif"
        alt="Plesk Disk and Bandwidth Usage"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Additionally, you can explore detailed statistics for FTP, Data Transfer, and Web Statistics within the same section.
