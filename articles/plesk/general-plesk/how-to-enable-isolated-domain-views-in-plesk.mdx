---
title: "Enable Isolated Domain Views in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to enable Isolated Domain Views in Plesk to simplify domain management and prevent errors when working with multiple websites."
---

<Info>
In Plesk, you can simplify domain management by enabling Isolated Domain Views. This feature ensures each domain opens on a separate page, allowing you to focus on one domain at a time, free from distractions or the risk of mistakes when managing multiple domains.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Websites & Domains**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif"
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set View to 'On a Separate Page'">
    On the right side of the screen, click on **Set View** and select the **On a Separate Page** option.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-view-mode-in-dynamic-list.gif"
        alt="Plesk view mode selection in dynamic list"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Turning on Isolated Domain Views in Plesk helps you stay focused and prevents mistakes, such as accidentally changing or deleting files in the wrong domain when working with multiple sites.
</Note>
