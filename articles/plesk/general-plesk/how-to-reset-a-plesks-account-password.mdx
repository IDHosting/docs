---
title: "Reset a Plesk Account Password"
description: "Follow these steps to reset your Plesk account password."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Plesk Login URL">
    Open your Plesk login URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Password Reset">
    Click on **Forget your password**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-login-page-forget-password.gif" 
        alt="Plesk login page with 'Forget your password' link highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials and Send">
    On the **Reset password** page, enter your **email address** or choose the **Username** option. Then type your username, and click **Send**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-reset-password-email.gif" 
        alt="Plesk password reset page with email and username fields"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    You should receive an email with the reset link. Please, open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set New Password">
    Enter the new password or click **Generate** to create one. You can copy it by clicking on the **view password** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-password-reset-final-step.gif" 
        alt="Plesk password reset final step with new password input and generate button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click on **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After resetting the password, you can log in to Plesk with the new one.
