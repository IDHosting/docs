---
title: "Change the Language of a Plesk Panel"
description: "Follow these steps to change the language of your Plesk Panel interface."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Account**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-click-on-account-client.gif"
        alt="Plesk interface with Account option highlighted in the sidebar menu."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access My Profile">
    Click on **My Profile** to access your user settings.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-myprofile-client.gif"
        alt="Plesk My Profile page with the profile options visible."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Plesk Language">
    Scroll down to the **Plesk language** section. Use the drop-down menu to select your desired language.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-client-change-language.gif"
        alt="Plesk language selection dropdown menu."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Changes">
    Click the **Apply** button to save your language preference.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-apply-client.gif"
        alt="Plesk interface with the Apply button highlighted."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

The language in your Plesk account has now been successfully changed.
