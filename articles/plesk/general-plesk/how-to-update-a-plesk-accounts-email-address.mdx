---
title: "Update a Plesk Account's Email Address"
description: "Learn how to update the primary and external email addresses associated with your Plesk account."
---

Follow these steps to change your Plesk contact email address.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Navigate to your Plesk login page and enter your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Account Settings">
    Once logged in, click on **Account** from the Sidebar Menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-click-on-account-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk sidebar menu with Account highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to My Profile">
    In the Account section, click on **My Profile**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-myprofile-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Account page with My Profile highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Email Address">
    Enter your new primary email address in the **Email Address** field. If you manage multiple domains, select the appropriate one from the dropdown. You can also update the **external email address**, which is used for password reset purposes.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/change-plesk-contact-email-address.gif" 
        alt="Plesk My Profile page with Email Address and External Email Address fields"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Changes">
    Click on the **Apply** button to save your changes.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-apply-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk My Profile page with Apply button highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You will see a success message confirming that your Plesk contact email address has been updated.
