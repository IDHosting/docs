---
title: "Update the Email Address of a Scheduled Task in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to update the email address associated with Scheduled Tasks (Cron Jobs) in your Plesk control panel."
---

Follow this guide to update the email address for your Scheduled Tasks (Cron Jobs) in Plesk.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Plesk">
    Access your Plesk account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Websites & Domains">
    From the sidebar menu, click on **Websites & Domains**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-websites-and-domains-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Websites & Domains menu item"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Scheduled Tasks">
    In the right sidebar, click the **Pull icon** and then select **Scheduled Tasks**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-scheduled-task-sidebar-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Scheduled Tasks sidebar menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate and Select Your Task">
    Find the task you wish to update in the **Command** list and click on it to edit.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-task-edit.gif" 
        alt="Plesk task edit view"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Email Address">
    Scroll down to the **Send notifications** section. Select **other users** and then update the email address in the provided field.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-scheduletalk-email-update.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Scheduled Task email update"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click **OK** to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If the <strong>Send notifications to</strong> option is not visible, ensure that either <strong>Every time</strong> or <strong>Errors only</strong> is selected under the <strong>Notify</strong> setting. This will make the email address field available.
</Note>
