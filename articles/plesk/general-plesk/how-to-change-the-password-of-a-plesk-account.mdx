---
title: "Change the Password of a Plesk Account"
description: "Follow these steps to change your Plesk account password."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk account by logging in with your current credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account Settings">
    Once logged in, click on **Account** from the Sidebar Menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-click-on-account-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk interface with 'Account' highlighted in the sidebar menu."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Profile Settings">
    In the Account section, click on **My Profile**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-myprofile-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk 'My Profile' page."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Locate the password field. You can either type in your desired new password or click the **Generate** button to create a strong, random password.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-password-change-enter.gif" 
        alt="Plesk password field with options to enter or generate a new password."
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Changes">
    After entering or generating your new password, click **OK** to save the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can now log in to your Plesk account using your newly set password.
