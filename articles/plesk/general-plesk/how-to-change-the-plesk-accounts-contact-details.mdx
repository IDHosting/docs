---
title: "Change Plesk Account Contact Details"
description: "Learn how to update your contact information within your Plesk account."
---

Follow these steps to change your Plesk account's contact details.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account">
    From the sidebar menu, click on **Account**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-click-on-account-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk sidebar menu with Account highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access My Profile">
    Click on **My Profile**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-myprofile-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Account page with My Profile option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Contact Details">
    Click on the **Contact Details** tab.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-client-contact-details-tab.gif" 
        alt="Plesk My Profile page with Contact Details tab"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Your Information">
    Modify the fields to update your contact details as needed.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-client-myprofile-modify-contact-details.gif" 
        alt="Plesk Contact Details form with fields to update"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Changes">
    Click the **Apply** button to save your updated information.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-apply-client.gif" 
        alt="Plesk page with Apply button highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful submission, you will see a confirmation message indicating that your Plesk contact details have been updated.
