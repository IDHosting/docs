---
title: "Delete a Database in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to delete a database in Plesk when it's no longer needed."
---

<Info>
This tutorial will guide you through the process of deleting a database in Plesk.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Databases">
    From the **Sidebar Menu**, click on **Databases**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-database-menu.gif"
        alt="Plesk Databases Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Database">
    Locate the database you wish to remove. Click the **trash icon** next to it and confirm the deletion by clicking **Yes**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/plesk-remove-database-final.gif"
        alt="Plesk Remove Database Confirmation"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

The database has now been successfully deleted from your Plesk account.
