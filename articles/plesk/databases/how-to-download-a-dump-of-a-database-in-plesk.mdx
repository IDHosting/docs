---
title: "Download a Dump of a Database in Plesk"
description: "Learn how to generate and download a backup of your database using Plesk."
---

You can generate a backup of your database and download it to your computer using Plesk.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Plesk account">
    Access your Plesk control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Databases">
    Click on **Databases** from the Sidebar Menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-database-menu.gif" 
        alt="Plesk database menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your database">
    Choose the database you want to download from the **list of databases**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-database-list.gif" 
        alt="Plesk database list"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Export Dump">
    Click on **Export Dump**. A popup box will appear.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-export-database.gif" 
        alt="Plesk export database"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and Export">
    Choose the directory where your database dump will be saved. Check the box for **Automatically download the dump after its creation**, and then confirm by clicking **Export Dump**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/plesk-database-export-dump.gif" 
        alt="Plesk database export dump configuration"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once the database dump is generated, your browser should prompt you to download it. If the download doesn't start automatically, click the **Download** button.
