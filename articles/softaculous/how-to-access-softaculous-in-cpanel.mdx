---
title: "Access Softaculous in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to access and use Softaculous, a one-click script installer, directly from your cPanel account."
---

Softaculous is a powerful one-click script installer that simplifies the process of installing various web applications onto your website. This guide will walk you through accessing Softaculous directly from your cPanel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Begin by logging into your cPanel account using your provided credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Softaculous Apps Installer">
    Once logged in, scroll down to the **Software** section. Here, you will find and click on the **Softaculous Apps Installer** icon.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/softaculous-icon.gif"
        alt="Softaculous Apps Installer icon in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Softaculous Interface">
    Clicking the Softaculous Apps Installer icon will open the Softaculous interface within your cPanel. From here, you can browse and install a wide range of applications.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/cp-softaculous-interface.gif"
        alt="Softaculous interface within cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to cPanel (Optional)">
    If you need to navigate back to your main cPanel dashboard, you can do so by clicking on the **cPanel** icon.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/cp-softaculous-goback.gif"
        alt="cPanel icon to return to the main dashboard"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully accessed the Softaculous Apps Installer. You can proceed to explore and install your desired web applications.
