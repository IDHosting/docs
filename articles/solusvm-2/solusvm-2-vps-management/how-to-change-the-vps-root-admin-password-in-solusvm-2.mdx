---
title: "Change the VPS Root/Admin Password in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to change the root/admin password for your VPS using the SolusVM 2 control panel."
---

You can change the root/admin password of your VPS Operating System using SolusVM 2.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Log in to your SolusVM 2 Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Project">
    Under **Projects**, click on the **project** that contains your VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS Status screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your VPS">
    Click on the appropriate VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-select.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS selection screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Boot & Rescue">
    Go to the **Boot & Rescue** tab.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-boot-rescue-tab.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Boot & Rescue tab"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset Root Password">
    Click **Reset Root Password**, then click **Reset** to proceed.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-reset-root-password.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Reset Root Password button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Password Change">
    It may take a few moments for the password to change, after which a success message will display. Copy the new password to a secure location, such as Notepad.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-password-changed.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Password Changed success message"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have successfully changed the root/admin password for your VPS.
