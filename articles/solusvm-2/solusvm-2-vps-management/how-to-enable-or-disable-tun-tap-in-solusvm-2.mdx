---
title: "Enable or Disable TUN/TAP in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to enable or disable TUN/TAP for your VPS within the SolusVM 2 control panel."
---

SolusVM 2 allows you to easily enable or disable TUN/TAP functionality for your Virtual Private Servers (VPS). This guide will walk you through the process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Begin by logging into your SolusVM 2 Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Project">
    Under the **Projects** section, click on the project that contains the VPS you wish to manage.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS Status page"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your VPS">
    Click on the specific VPS you want to configure from the list.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-select.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS selection"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Once you have selected your VPS, click on the **Settings** tab.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-settings-tab.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Settings tab"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable TUN/TAP">
    To enable TUN/TAP, toggle the **TUN/TAP** option to the 'on' position. Then, click the **Change** button to save your selection.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-tun-tap-enabled.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 TUN/TAP toggle enabled"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reboot Your VPS">
    After making the change, it is necessary to reboot your VPS for the new settings to take effect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Disabling TUN/TAP

To disable TUN/TAP, follow the same steps above, but in Step 5, toggle the **TUN/TAP** option to the 'off' position. After clicking **Change**, remember to reboot your VPS.

You have now successfully learned how to enable or disable TUN/TAP in SolusVM 2.
