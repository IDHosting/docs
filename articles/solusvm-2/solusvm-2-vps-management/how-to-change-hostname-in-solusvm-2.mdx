---
title: "Change Hostname in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to change the hostname of your VPS within the SolusVM 2 control panel."
---

You can easily change the hostname of your Virtual Private Server (VPS) using the SolusVM 2 control panel. Follow the steps below to update your hostname.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Access your SolusVM 2 control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Project">
    Navigate to the **Projects** section and click on the project that contains the VPS you wish to modify.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif" alt="SolusVM 2 VPS Status" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select VPS">
    Click on the specific VPS from the list to access its details.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-select.gif" alt="SolusVM 2 Select VPS" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Hostname">
    Locate and click the **Edit** icon, typically found in the top-right corner. In the provided field, enter your desired new **hostname**. Once entered, click **Save**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/solusvm2-hostname-change.gif" alt="SolusVM 2 Change Hostname" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reboot Required">
    A **reboot** of your VPS is necessary for the hostname changes to take effect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here are some examples of valid hostnames:
- `server1.example.com`
- `hostingserver.example.com`

You have now successfully changed the hostname for your VPS in SolusVM 2.
