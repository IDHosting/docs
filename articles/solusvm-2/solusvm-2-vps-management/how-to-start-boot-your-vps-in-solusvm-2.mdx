---
title: "Start/Boot Your VPS in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to start or boot your Virtual Private Server (VPS) if it is offline using the SolusVM 2 control panel."
---

If your VPS is down or offline, you can easily start it using the SolusVM 2 control panel. Follow these steps to get your VPS back online.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Access your SolusVM 2 control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Project">
    Once logged in, navigate to the **Projects** section and click on the specific **project** that contains the VPS you wish to start.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS status screen showing projects"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your VPS">
    Within the selected project, click on the specific VPS you want to start.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-select.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 interface showing a list of VPS instances to select from"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the VPS">
    On the VPS management page, locate and click the **Start** button.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-start-vps.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS management page with the Start button highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>

    <Note>
      It may take a few moments for the VPS to fully start and become accessible after clicking the Start button.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
