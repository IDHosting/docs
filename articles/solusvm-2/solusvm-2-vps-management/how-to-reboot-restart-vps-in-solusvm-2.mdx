---
title: "Reboot/Restart VPS in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to reboot or restart your Virtual Private Server (VPS) using either the SolusVM 2 control panel or the command line."
---

You can restart your VPS through the SolusVM 2 control panel or via the command line.

## How to Reboot/Restart a VPS via SolusVM 2?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SolusVM 2 Control Panel">
    Access your SolusVM 2 control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Project">
    Under **Projects**, click on the **project** that contains your VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS Status"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your VPS">
    Click on the specific VPS you wish to restart.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-select.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 Select VPS"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Restart">
    Click on the **Restart** button, typically located at the top right.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-restart-icon.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 Restart Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Restart">
    Choose **Yes** to confirm the restart action.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-reboot-confirmation.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 Reboot Confirmation"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    The VPS will begin its reboot process. This may take some time. Once completed, a confirmation message will be displayed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Reboot/Restart a VPS via Command Line?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in via SSH">
    Connect to your VPS using an SSH client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute Reboot Command">
    Enter the following command and press Enter:
    ```bash
    shutdown -r now
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

After executing the command, your VPS will begin to reboot. This process can take a few minutes.

<Warning>
If your VPS does not become accessible online within 5 to 10 minutes after initiating a command-line reboot, it is recommended to perform the restart through the SolusVM 2 control panel to ensure proper functionality.
</Warning>
