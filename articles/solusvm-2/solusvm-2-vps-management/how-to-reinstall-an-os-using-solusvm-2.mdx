---
title: "Reinstall an OS Using SolusVM 2"
description: "A guide on how to reinstall the operating system on your VPS using the SolusVM 2 control panel."
---

You can easily reinstall the operating system on your VPS using SolusVM 2. Please be aware that this process will erase all data currently on your VPS.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Log in to your SolusVM 2 Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Project">
    Under **Projects**, click on the **project** that contains your VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS Status"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your VPS">
    Click on the specific VPS you wish to manage.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-select.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Select VPS"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Reinstall">
    Click on the **Reinstall** option.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-reinstall-option.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Reinstall Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Operating System">
    In the **Operating System** section, select your desired OS. If you need a specific version, click **Other Versions** and choose it from the list.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-select-os-version.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Select OS Version"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset and Save Password">
    Tick the **Reset password** option. Click the **clipboard icon** to copy the generated password and save it in a secure location, such as Notepad. You will need this password to access your VPS after the reinstallation.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-os-reset-password.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 OS Reset Password"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Reinstallation">
    Scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Reinstall**. A confirmation prompt will appear; click **Reinstall** again to proceed.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-reinstall-button.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Reinstall Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    A message will indicate that the installation may take up to 10 minutes. Remember to save the copied password, as it's crucial for accessing your VPS post-reinstallation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Reinstalling your OS will permanently delete all data on your VPS. Ensure you have backed up any critical information before proceeding.
</Note>
<Info>
  The OS reinstallation process typically takes up to 10 minutes to complete.
</Info>
