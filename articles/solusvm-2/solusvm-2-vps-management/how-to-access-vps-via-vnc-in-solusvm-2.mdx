---
title: "Access VPS via VNC in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to access your VPS using VNC in SolusVM 2 for emergency situations or OS installations."
---

You can access your VPS via VNC in an emergency, such as when a firewall blocks your IP, the internet connection on the VPS is not working, or when you cannot access SSH. You can also use VNC for OS installation or reinstallation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Log in to your SolusVM 2 Control Panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Project">
    Under **Projects**, click on the **project** that contains your VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS Status"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select VPS">
    Click on the appropriate VPS.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-select.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Select VPS"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open VNC Dialog">
    Click on the **VNC dialog** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vnc-start.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Start VNC"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access VNC Area">
    The VNC Area will resemble the following screenshot:
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vnc-area.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 VNC Area"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Now you know how to use VNC to access your VPS in SolusVM 2.
