---
title: "Disable Two-Factor Authentication in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to disable two-factor authentication from your SolusVM 2 account."
---

Follow these instructions to disable two-factor authentication from your SolusVM 2 account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SolusVM 2 Control Panel">
    Access your SolusVM 2 control panel by navigating to its login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account Settings">
    Once logged in, click on **Account** in the left-hand sidebar.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-account-menu.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Account Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Two-Factor Authentication Settings">
    In the Account section, click on **Two-Factor Authentication**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-two-factor-menu.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Two-Factor Authentication Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable Two-Factor Authentication">
    Click the **Disable** button. You will be prompted to confirm this action.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-two-factor-disable.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Disable Two-Factor Authentication Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate Your Request">
    SolusVM will prompt you for authentication. Enter your SolusVM password to confirm the disabling of two-factor authentication.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You should see a success message indicating that two-factor authentication has been disabled.

<Note>
  For continued account security, it is recommended to re-enable two-factor authentication at a later time.
</Note>
