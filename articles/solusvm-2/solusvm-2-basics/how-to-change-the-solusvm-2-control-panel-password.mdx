---
title: "Change the SolusVM 2 Control Panel Password"
description: "Learn how to change your SolusVM 2 control panel password to maintain account security."
---

If you have access to your SolusVM 2 VPS control panel, you can easily change your password. Regularly changing your password is a recommended security practice.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SolusVM 2 Control Panel">
    Access your SolusVM 2 control panel by navigating to its URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account Settings">
    In the left-hand sidebar, click on the **Account** menu item.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-account-menu.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Account Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password Details">
    Under the **Account** section, you will find fields to update your password:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Current Password:</strong> Enter your existing SolusVM 2 control panel password.</li>
      <li><strong>Change Password:</strong> Enter your desired new password.</li>
      <li><strong>Confirm Password:</strong> Re-enter your new password to confirm.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-password-change.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 Password Change Fields"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Settings">
    Once you have entered your new password details, click on the <strong>Apply Settings</strong> button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully changed your SolusVM 2 control panel password.
