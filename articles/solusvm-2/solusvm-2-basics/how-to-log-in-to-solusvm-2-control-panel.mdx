You can find the Control Panel URL, username (email address), and password in your VPS welcome email.

## SolusVM 2 Login Details Example

*   **Control Panel URL:** `https://server-hostname/login` or use the IP address.
*   **Username:** Your email address.
*   **Password:** Your password.

## How to Log In to SolusVM 2 Control Panel?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open VPS Control Panel">
    Navigate to the Control Panel URL provided in your welcome email. This can be either a hostname or an IP address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Login Credentials">
    Enter your SolusVM email address and password in the respective fields.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-login-page.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 login page with fields for email and password"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click on the **Sign In** button. You will be redirected to the SolusVM 2 VPS management area.
  </Step>
</Steps>
