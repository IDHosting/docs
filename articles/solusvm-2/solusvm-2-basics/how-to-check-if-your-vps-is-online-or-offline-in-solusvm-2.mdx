You can easily check the status of your SolusVM Virtual Private Server (VPS) to determine if it is online or offline. This information can be accessed directly from the SolusVM 2 Control Panel.

## Checking VPS Status

Follow these steps to check your VPS status:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Access your SolusVM 2 Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Project">
    Under the **Projects** section, click on the specific **project** that contains the VPS you wish to check.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-vps-status.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 project selection screen"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Server Status">
    Navigate to the **servers** tab. Here, you will see the status of your VPS. If your VPS is online, its status will be displayed as **Running**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-running-status.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 server status showing Running"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully learned how to check your VPS status in SolusVM 2.
