---
title: "Enable Two-Factor Authentication in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to secure your SolusVM 2 account with two-factor authentication."
---

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an essential layer of security to your SolusVM 2 account. It requires a unique code from an authentication app during login, preventing unauthorized access even if your password is compromised.

## Requirements

To enable 2FA, you will need:

*   A smartphone with a supported time-based one-time password (TOTP) app. Popular options are available for Android, iOS, and Windows mobile operating systems.

## Enabling Two-Factor Authentication

Follow these steps to enable 2FA on your SolusVM 2 account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SolusVM 2">
    Access your SolusVM 2 Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account Settings">
    In the left-hand sidebar, click on **Account**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-account-menu.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 account menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Two-Factor Authentication">
    Click on the **Two-Factor Authentication** option.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-two-factor-menu.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 two-factor authentication menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code or Enter Secret Key">
    Open your authenticator app (e.g., Google Authenticator) and scan the displayed QR code. If your app does not support QR code scanning, you can manually enter the provided secret key.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-two-factor-enable.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 two-factor authentication QR code and secret key"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Authentication Code and Enable">
    In the **Code from the app** field, enter the current code displayed in your authenticator app. Then, click **Enable**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/solusvm2-enable-two-fa.gif" 
        alt="SolusVM 2 enable two-factor authentication code entry"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm with SolusVM Password">
    You will be prompted to enter your SolusVM password to confirm the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Upon successful activation, SolusVM will display a recovery key. This key is crucial for regaining access to your account if you lose access to your authenticator device. Copy this recovery key and store it securely in a safe place.
</Note>
