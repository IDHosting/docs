---
title: "Check VPS Specifications and Virtualization Info in SolusVM 2"
description: "Learn how to easily check your VPS specifications, including RAM, disk space, virtualization type, and IP address, directly from the SolusVM 2 Control Panel."
---

You can check VPS specifications such as RAM, disk space, virtualization, IP Address, and more from the SolusVM 2 Control Panel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SolusVM 2 Control Panel">
    Access your SolusVM 2 Control Panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Project">
    Under the **Projects** section, click on the project that contains the VPS you wish to check.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/svm2/solusvm2-vps-status.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 project list showing VPS status"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your VPS">
    Click on the specific VPS you want to view from the list within the project.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/svm2/solusvm2-vps-select.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 project view showing a list of VPS instances"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View VPS Specifications">
    Once you have selected your VPS, you will see its specifications displayed directly under the VPS hostname. This includes information such as the VPS IP address, operating system disk size, available RAM, and other crucial details.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/svm2/solusvm2-vps-specification.gif"
        alt="SolusVM 2 VPS details page showing specifications like IP address, disk space, and RAM"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

This guide should help you quickly find the information you need about your VPS.
