---
title: "Access Softaculous in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to access the Softaculous one-click script installer through your SiteWorx control panel."
---

Softaculous is a one-click script installer that automates the installation of web applications to your website. You can easily access it via your SiteWorx account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Softaculous">
    In the **Additional Features** section of your SiteWorx dashboard, click on the **Softaculous** icon.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-softaculous-icon.gif"
        alt="Softaculous icon in SiteWorx Additional Features"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Softaculous Interface">
    SiteWorx will automatically open the Softaculous interface, allowing you to browse and install various web applications.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/siteworx-softaculous-interface.gif"
        alt="Softaculous interface within SiteWorx"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>

    <Info>
      If you wish to return to your SiteWorx control panel, click on the **Go to Control Panel** icon within the Softaculous interface.
      <Frame>
        <img
          src="/images/kb/sw-softaculous-goback.gif"
          alt="Go to Control Panel icon in Softaculous"
          style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
        />
      </Frame>
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
