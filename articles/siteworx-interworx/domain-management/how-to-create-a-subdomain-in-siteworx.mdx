---
title: "Create a Subdomain in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to create a subdomain within your SiteWorx account."
---

A subdomain is an additional part of your primary domain, such as `blog.example.com`. This tutorial will guide you through the process of creating a subdomain in SiteWorx.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SiteWorx">
    Begin by logging in to your SiteWorx account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Subdomain Settings">
    Once logged in, navigate to the **Domains** section and click on **Subdomain**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-subdomain-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Subdomain Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Subdomain">
    Click on the **Plus** icon or the **Add a Subdomain** button to start creating a new subdomain.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-subdomain-plus-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Add Subdomain Plus Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Subdomain Details">
    Enter your desired subdomain name. If you have multiple domains associated with your account, select the correct domain from the drop-down menu. Finally, click **Add**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/subdomain-add.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Subdomain Add Configuration"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful creation, you should see a confirmation message indicating that your subdomain has been created.
