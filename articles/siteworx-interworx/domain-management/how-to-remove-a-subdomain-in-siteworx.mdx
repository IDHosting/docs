---
title: "Remove a Subdomain in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to remove a subdomain from your SiteWorx account."
---

A subdomain is an additional part of your primary domain. Follow these steps to remove a subdomain in SiteWorx.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Subdomain settings">
    In the **Domains** section, click on the **Subdomain** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-subdomain-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Subdomain Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Delete the Subdomain">
    Choose the subdomain you wish to remove from the list. Select **Delete** from the dropdown menu, and then click **Go**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/subdomain-delete.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Subdomain Delete Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    A confirmation window will appear. You can choose to **Delete domain directory** or leave it. Click **Delete** to finalize the removal.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/subdomain-delete-final.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Subdomain Deletion Confirmation"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    You will see a success message indicating that the subdomain has been removed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
