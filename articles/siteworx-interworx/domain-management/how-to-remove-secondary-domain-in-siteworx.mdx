---
title: "Remove Secondary Domain in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to easily remove a secondary domain from your SiteWorx account."
---

Removing a secondary domain in SiteWorx is a straightforward process. It is highly recommended to back up your account before proceeding with any domain removal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx Account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Domain Management">
    In the **Domains** section, click on the **Manage** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-manage-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Manage Domains Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Delete the Domain">
    Locate the secondary domain you wish to remove from the list. From the drop-down menu next to the domain, select **Delete**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/secondary-domain-delete.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Secondary Domain Delete Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    After selecting **Delete**, click the **Go** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Click **Delete** to finalize the removal of the secondary domain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful completion, you will see a confirmation message indicating that your domain has been removed.
