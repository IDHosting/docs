---
title: "Add a Secondary Domain in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to add a secondary domain to your SiteWorx account."
---

Secondary domains function as independent domains with their own virtual hosts. This guide will walk you through the process of adding one in SiteWorx.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SiteWorx">
    Begin by logging into your SiteWorx account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Domain Management">
    In the **Domains** section, click on **Manage**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-manage-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Manage Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Domain">
    Click on the **Plus** icon or the **Add Domain** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-subdomain-plus-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Add Domain Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Domain Details">
    Fill in the following information:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Domain</strong>: Enter the domain name you wish to add.</li>
      <li><strong>PHP Version</strong>: Select the desired PHP version for this domain.</li>
      <li><strong>IPv4 Address</strong>: Choose an IP address or leave the default setting.</li>
      <li><strong>IPv6 Address</strong>: Leave this as is or select your IPv6 address.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/secondary-domain-add.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Secondary Domain Add Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Addition">
    Click the **Add** button to finalize the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful addition, you will see a confirmation message indicating that your domain has been added.
