---
title: "Create or Remove the Email Autoresponder in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to set up an automated email response for your SiteWorx account or remove an existing one."
---

You can use the email autoresponder for an automated response to your email subscribers or senders. It can prove very helpful when you are on vacation or in similar circumstances.

## Create an Email Autoresponder

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Autoresponders">
    In the **Email** section, click on **Autoresponders**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/sw-autoresponders-icon.gif" alt="SiteWorx Autoresponders Icon" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Autoresponder">
    Click on the **Plus** icon or **Add an Email Autoresponder**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/sw-subdomain-plus-icon.gif" alt="Add Autoresponder Icon" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Autoresponder Details">
    Fill in the following fields:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Email Address</strong>: Enter the email username for which you want to enable the autoresponder. For example, if you want to use it on `john@example.com`, enter `John`. Choose the appropriate domain from the drop-down menu if you have multiple domains.</li>
      <li><strong>Send copy to</strong>: Optionally, enter an email address where you want to receive a copy of the auto-replied emails.</li>
      <li><strong>Autoresponder Message</strong>: Compose your automated response message (e.g., your vacation message).</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/autoresponder-siteworx-email.gif" alt="SiteWorx Autoresponder Email Configuration" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Autoresponder">
    Click on the **Add** button to save your settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Remove an Email Autoresponder

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Autoresponder Settings">
    Log in to your SiteWorx account and navigate to the **Email** section, then click on **Autoresponders**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete Existing Autoresponder">
    Under the **Existing Autoresponders** section, click on the **Delete** icon next to the email address you wish to remove the autoresponder from.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/delete-autoresponder-siteworx.gif" alt="SiteWorx Delete Autoresponder Icon" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Confirm the deletion by clicking **Delete** when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
