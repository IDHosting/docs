---
title: "Remove an Email Account in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to remove an email account from your SiteWorx control panel."
---

Follow these steps to remove an email account from your SiteWorx account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mailboxes">
    In the **Email** section, click on the **Mailboxes** icon.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-mailboxes-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Mailboxes Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Delete Email Address">
    Locate the email address you wish to remove. From the drop-down menu next to it, select **Delete**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/delete-email-address.gif" 
        alt="Delete Email Address Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click the **Go** button, and then confirm your action by clicking **Delete** in the confirmation prompt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful deletion, you will see a confirmation message indicating that your email address has been removed.
