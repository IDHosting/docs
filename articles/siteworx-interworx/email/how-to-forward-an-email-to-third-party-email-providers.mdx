---
title: "Forward an Email to Third-party Email Providers"
description: "Learn how to set up email forwarding in SiteWorx to direct incoming emails to an external email address."
---

This guide will walk you through the process of setting up email forwarding for your SiteWorx account, allowing you to receive emails at a different address.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SiteWorx">
    Begin by logging into your SiteWorx account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Aliases">
    In the **Email** section, click on **Aliases**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-aliases-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Aliases Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Alias">
    Click on the **Plus** icon or the **Add an Alias** button.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-subdomain-plus-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Add Alias Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Forwarding Details">
    Enter the following details:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Email Address</strong>: Enter the username for the email address you want to forward (e.g., `john`). If you have multiple domains, select the appropriate domain from the dropdown.</li>
      <li><strong>Forwards To</strong>: Enter the external email address where you want to receive the forwarded emails.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-email-aliases-add.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Email Aliases Add Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Add">
    Click on the **Add** button to save the forwarding settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully set up email forwarding. All incoming messages to the specified email address will be automatically forwarded to the external address you provided.
