---
title: "Access Email from SiteWorx Webmail"
description: "Learn how to access your email account directly through SiteWorx webmail."
---

You can access your email account from Webmail by following these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Navigate to your SiteWorx login page and enter your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Webmail">
    In the **Email** section of your SiteWorx dashboard, click on the **Webmail** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-webmail-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Webmail Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to Webmail">
    Enter your full email address and password in the provided fields, then click **Login**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/webmail-page.gif" 
        alt="Webmail Login Page"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once logged in, you can manage your email account directly from the webmail reader interface.
