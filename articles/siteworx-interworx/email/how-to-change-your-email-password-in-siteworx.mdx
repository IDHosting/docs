---
title: "Change Your Email Password in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to change your email password using the SiteWorx control panel."
---

Follow these steps to change your email password in SiteWorx:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mailboxes">
    In the **Email** section, click on **Mailboxes**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-mailboxes-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Mailboxes Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Email Account">
    Click on the **Pencil icon** next to the email account you wish to modify.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/change-email-password.gif"
        alt="Edit Email Account in SiteWorx"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    Enter your desired new password in the provided field. Alternatively, click the **Magic Wand** icon to generate a strong, random password.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-change-password.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Change Password Interface"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Save** button to confirm your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful completion, you will see a success message indicating that your email password has been updated.
