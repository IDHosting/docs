---
title: "Create an Email Account in SiteWorx"
description: "A guide on how to create a new email account using SiteWorx."
---

Follow these steps to create a new email account in SiteWorx:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mailboxes">
    In the **Email** section, click on **Mailboxes**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-mailboxes-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Mailboxes Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Email Box">
    Click on the **Plus** icon or the **Add an Email Box** button.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-subdomain-plus-icon.gif"
        alt="Add Email Box Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email Account Details">
    Fill in the following fields:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>E-mail Address</strong>: Enter the desired username for your email account (e.g., your first name). If you manage multiple domains, select the correct domain from the dropdown list.</li>
      <li><strong>Password</strong>: Set a password for the email account. You can also click the **Magic Wand** icon to generate a strong password.</li>
      <li><strong>Disk Space Quota</strong>: Specify the storage limit for the mailbox in megabytes, or choose **Unlimited**.</li>
      <li><strong>Send Copy To</strong>: This field is optional. If you enter email addresses here, a copy of all incoming messages will be forwarded to them.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/create-email-address.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Create Email Address Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Creation">
    Click the **Add** button to create the email account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once created, your new email account will be listed under **Existing E-mail Boxes**.

<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/kb/existing-email-list.gif"
    alt="SiteWorx Existing Email Boxes List"
    style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
  />
</Frame>

Your email account is now active and ready to send and receive messages.
