---
title: "Update Your SiteWorx Email Address"
description: "Learn how to change your email address associated with your SiteWorx account."
---

You can update your SiteWorx email address by following the steps below.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to User Accounts">
    In the **Administration** section, click on **User Accounts**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-user-accounts-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx User Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the Email Account">
    Click on the **Pencil icon** next to the email account you wish to modify.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-user-account-edit.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx User Account Edit"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Email Address">
    In the **Email** field, type your new email address.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/email-edit.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Email Edit Field"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Save** button to confirm the update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your email address has now been successfully changed.
