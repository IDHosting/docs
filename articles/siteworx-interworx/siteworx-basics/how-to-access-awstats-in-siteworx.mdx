---
title: "Access AWStats in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to access and use AWStats within your SiteWorx account to view website analytics."
---

AWStats is an open-source web analytics tool that provides detailed insights into your website's traffic. Follow these steps to access your AWStats reports in SiteWorx.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to AWStats">
    In the **Statistics** section of your SiteWorx dashboard, click on the **AWStats** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-awstats-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx AWStats icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your domain (if applicable)">
    If you manage multiple domains, select the desired domain from the drop-down menu and click **Login**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the statistics type">
    Under the **Stats Type** section, click on the available statistic option you wish to view.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/siteworx-awstats-option.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx AWStats statistics type selection"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once these steps are completed, the AWStats interface will open, allowing you to examine your website's detailed statistics.
