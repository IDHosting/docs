---
title: "Access Webalizer in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to access and use Webalizer, an open-source web analytics tool, within your SiteWorx account to view website statistics."
---

Webalizer is an open-source web analytics tool that provides insights into your website's traffic. Follow these steps to access Webalizer in your SiteWorx account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Webalizer">
    Once logged in, locate the **Statistics** section in the SiteWorx interface. Within this section, click on the **Webalizer** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-webalizer-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Webalizer icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Webalizer Statistics">
    Your Webalizer statistics will automatically open in a new browser window, providing you with detailed analytics for your website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Now you know how to access Webalizer in SiteWorx and check your website statistics easily.
