---
title: "Change the Language in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to change the language settings in your SiteWorx account."
---

SiteWorx supports multiple languages. Follow this guide to change your SiteWorx language to your preferred native language.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to User Accounts">
    In the **Administration** section, click on **User Accounts**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-user-accounts-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx User Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your SiteWorx Account">
    Click on the **Pencil icon** next to your SiteWorx account to edit its settings.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-user-account-edit.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx User Account Edit"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Save Language">
    From the **Language** drop-down menu, select your desired language and then click the **Save** button.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/language-change.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Language Change Dropdown"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your SiteWorx language settings have now been successfully updated.
