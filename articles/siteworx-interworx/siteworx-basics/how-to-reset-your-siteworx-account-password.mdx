---
title: "Reset Your SiteWorx Account Password"
description: "Learn how to reset your SiteWorx account password by following these simple steps."
---

Follow these steps to reset your SiteWorx account password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx account by logging in with your current credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to User Accounts">
    In the **Administration** section, click on **User Accounts**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-user-accounts-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx User Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit User Account">
    Click on the **Pencil icon** next to the account you wish to edit.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-user-account-edit.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx User Account Edit"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set New Password">
    Enter your desired new password in the provided field. Alternatively, you can click the **Magic Wand** icon to generate a strong, random password.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-password-reset.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Password Reset Interface"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Save** button to confirm your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can now log in to SiteWorx using your newly created password.

<Note>
  If you have forgotten your SiteWorx password and are unable to log in to your account, please open a support ticket with us for further assistance.
</Note>
