---
title: "Delete an FTP Account in SiteWorx"
description: "Learn how to delete an FTP account within your SiteWorx control panel."
---

This article will guide you through the process of deleting an FTP account in SiteWorx.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to FTP Accounts">
    In the **FTP** section, click on **Accounts**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-ftp-accounts-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx FTP Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select and Delete the FTP Account">
    Locate the FTP account you wish to remove. From the drop-down menu next to the account, select **Delete**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/siteworx-ftp-delete.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx FTP Delete Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click on the **Go** button, and then confirm the deletion by clicking **Delete** in the confirmation prompt.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful deletion, you will see a confirmation message indicating that the FTP account has been removed.
