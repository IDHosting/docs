---
title: "Restore Partial Backup in SiteWorx"
description: "A guide on how to restore a partial backup in SiteWorx, overwriting existing data."
---

You can easily restore a SiteWorx partial backup. Please note that restoring such a backup will overwrite your existing data.

<Note>Restoring a partial backup will overwrite your existing data. Ensure you have a full backup before proceeding if necessary.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your SiteWorx account">
    Access your SiteWorx control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Backup Management">
    In the **Backups** section, click on **Management**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-backup-management-icon.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Backup Management Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Partial Backup Restore">
    Click on the three dots menu icon and choose **Restore from a partial backup**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/sw-partial-backup-restore-menu.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Partial Backup Restore Menu Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Restore Method">
    Choose the appropriate option from the drop-down menu and click **Continue**.

    <Info>If you have a partial backup file stored on your computer, select **Upload a partial backup file**. Then, click **Browse**, select the file from your local system, and confirm by clicking **Restore**.</Info>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/backup-restore-dropdown.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Backup Restore Dropdown Menu"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Restore">
    Click on **Restore** in the confirmation pop-up.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/restore-popup.gif" 
        alt="SiteWorx Restore Confirmation Pop-up"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully restored the partial backup. Your selected data should be back in place.
