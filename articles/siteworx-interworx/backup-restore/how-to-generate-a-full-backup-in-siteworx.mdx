---
title: "Generate a Full Backup in SiteWorx"
description: "Follow this tutorial to create a full website backup using SiteWorx."
---

Backing up your website regularly is crucial. This guide will walk you through the process of generating a full backup of your site using SiteWorx.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to SiteWorx">
    Access your SiteWorx account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Backup Now">
    In the **Backups** section, click on the **Backup Now** option.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/sw-backup-icon.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx Backup Now icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Backup Options">
    Choose your backup type and destination, then click **Continue**.
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Backup Type: </strong>Select <strong>Full Backup</strong>.</li>
      <li><strong>Backup Where: </strong>Choose <strong>Default Location</strong> or another preferred option.</li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/backup-generated.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx backup options selection"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Backup Details">
    Provide the necessary information for your backup:
    <ul>
      <li>
        <strong>E-mail status to:</strong> Enter your email address to receive a completion notification.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Domain Options</strong>: Select whether to back up a <strong>Single Domain</strong> or <strong>Multi Domain</strong> (all domains).
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Backup Where</strong>: If backing up to a remote location (FTP, SFTP, SCP), enter the required login credentials.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/backup-done.gif"
        alt="SiteWorx backup details entry"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Backup">
    Click the **Backup** button to start the backup process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After initiating the backup, you will be redirected to the **Backup Management** page. Here, you can monitor the progress of your backup and download it once it's complete. You will also receive an email notification when the SiteWorx backup has finished.
