---
title: "Edit an Account Level/Global Email Filter in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to edit account-level or global email filters in cPanel to manage unwanted emails across all your accounts."
---

Editing an account-level or global email filter in cPanel is a straightforward process that helps you manage unwanted emails across all your email accounts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Begin by logging in to your cPanel account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Global Email Filters">
    In the **Email** section, click on **Global Email Filters**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/account-level-filtering-icon.gif"
        alt="Global Email Filters icon in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Filter to Edit">
    Under the **Current Filters** section, you will see a list of all existing filters. Click **Edit** next to the filter you wish to modify.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/edit-filter.gif"
        alt="Editing an existing email filter in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Desired Changes">
    Modify the filter rules according to your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Once you have made the necessary adjustments, click on the **Save** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully edited the global email filter in cPanel. Your updated rules will be applied to all email accounts under your domain.
