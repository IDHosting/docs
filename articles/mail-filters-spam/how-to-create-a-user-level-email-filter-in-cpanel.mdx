---
title: "Create a User Level Email Filter in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to create a user-level email filter in cPanel to manage spam and unwanted emails."
---

It is challenging to completely stop spam emails, but you can effectively filter, discard, or deliver them to a selected folder using cPanel's email filtering tools. This guide will walk you through creating a user-level email filter.

## How to Create a User Level Email Filter?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel account using your provided credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Filters">
    In the **Email** section, click on **Email Filters**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/user-level-filtering-icon.gif" 
        alt="Email Filters icon in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Filters for a User">
    Under the **Filters by Users** section, you will see a list of your existing email accounts. Click on **Manage Filters** next to the email account you wish to configure.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/manage-filter-by-user-level.gif" 
        alt="Manage Filters by User Level"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Filter">
    Click on the **Create a New Filter** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Filter">
    Enter a descriptive name for your filter in the **Filter Name** input box.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Filter Rules">
    You can set various rules to filter your emails. For example, to filter emails containing specific words like "Lottery," select **Body** from the first dropdown list and **Contains** from the second dropdown list. Then, enter the word "Lottery" in the text field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an Action">
    From the **Actions** dropdown menu, select the desired action. For this example, we will choose **Discard Message**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/create-filter-body.gif" 
        alt="Create Filter rule for Body Contains Lottery and Action Discard Message"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the Filter">
    Finally, click on the **Create** button to save your new filter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your user-level email filter is now active in cPanel and will help you manage your inbox more effectively by filtering unwanted messages.
