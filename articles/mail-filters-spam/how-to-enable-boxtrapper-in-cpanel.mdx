---
title: "Enable BoxTrapper in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to enable and configure BoxTrapper in cPanel to filter unwanted emails."
---

BoxTrapper is an email filtering tool that requires senders not on your whitelist to respond to a verification email before their message is delivered. This helps protect your inbox from spam.

You can enable BoxTrapper for all your email accounts or for specific ones.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel Account">
    Access your cPanel account using your provided credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to BoxTrapper">
    In the **Email** section of your cPanel dashboard, click on the **BoxTrapper** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/open-boxtrap-icon.gif"
        alt="BoxTrapper icon in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an Email Account">
    On the BoxTrapper page, you will see a list of your email accounts under the **Account** heading. Find the email account you wish to configure and click **Manage** next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/boxtrap-account-list.gif"
        alt="List of email accounts in BoxTrapper"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable BoxTrapper">
    On the BoxTrapper Manage page for the selected account, check the **Status**. If it shows as <span style={{ color: '#ff0000' }}><strong>Disabled</strong></span>, click the <span style={{ color: '#339966' }}><strong>Enable</strong></span> button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully enabled BoxTrapper for the selected email account in cPanel. It will begin filtering unwanted emails from your inbox.
