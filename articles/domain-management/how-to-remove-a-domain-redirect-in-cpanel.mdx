---
title: "Remove a Domain Redirect in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to easily remove an unwanted domain redirect in your cPanel account."
---

If a domain redirect is no longer needed, you can easily remove it in cPanel. This ensures visitors access the original website instead of being redirected elsewhere.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Begin by logging in to your cPanel account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Redirects">
    In the **Domains** section, click on **Redirects**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/cpanel-redirect.gif"
        alt="cPanel Redirects section"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate and Remove Redirect">
    Scroll down to the **Current Redirects** section. Find the domain you wish to remove the redirect from and click on **remove**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/remove-redirect.gif"
        alt="Remove redirect button in cPanel"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Removal">
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Click on **Remove Redirect** again to confirm the action.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/paper-domain-redirect-remove.gif"
        alt="Confirm removal of domain redirect"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully removed the domain redirect in cPanel.
