---
title: "Remove an Add-on Domain in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to remove an add-on domain from your cPanel account to free up space and maintain organization."
---

If you no longer need an add-on domain in cPanel, you can remove it easily to free up space and keep your account organized.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Access your cPanel account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Domains">
    In the **Domains** section, click on the **Domains** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cp-domains-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Domains Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate and Manage Domain">
    Find the add-on domain you wish to remove in the list. Click on the **Manage** button next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/remove-addon-domain.gif" 
        alt="Manage Add-on Domain"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Removal">
    Scroll down the page and click on the **Remove Domain** button. You will be prompted to confirm the removal. Click to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once confirmed, you should see a success message indicating that your add-on domain has been removed.
