---
title: "Remove a Subdomain in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to remove a subdomain from your cPanel account."
---

A subdomain is an additional part of your primary domain. This tutorial will guide you through the process of removing a subdomain in cPanel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel account by navigating to your cPanel login URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Domains section">
    In the **Domains** section of your cPanel dashboard, click on the **Domains** icon.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cp-domains-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel Domains icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate and manage the subdomain">
    Find the subdomain you wish to remove in the list. Click on the **Manage** button next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/remove-addon-domain.gif" 
        alt="Manage subdomain button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove the subdomain">
    Scroll down the page and click on the **Remove Domain** button. You will be prompted to confirm the removal. Click to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once confirmed, you should see a success message indicating that your subdomain has been successfully removed.
