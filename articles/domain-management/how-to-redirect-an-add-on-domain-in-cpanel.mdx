---
title: "Redirect an Add-on Domain in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to redirect your add-on domain to another domain using cPanel."
---

This article will guide you through the process of redirecting an add-on domain to another website using your cPanel account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to cPanel">
    Access your cPanel account by logging in with your provided credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Redirects">
    In the **Domains** section of your cPanel dashboard, click on **Redirects**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/cpanel-redirect.gif" alt="cPanel Redirects Interface" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the Redirect">
    Under the **Add Redirect** section, you will need to enter the following details:
    <ul>
      <li>
        <strong>Type:</strong> Select either <strong>Permanent (301)</strong> for a permanent move or <strong>Temporary (302)</strong> for a temporary redirect.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>https?://(www.)?:</strong> Choose the add-on domain you wish to redirect from the dropdown menu. You can also specify a particular page within that domain to redirect.
      </li>
      <li>
        <strong>Redirects to:</strong> Enter the full URL of the destination where you want your add-on domain to redirect.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/kb/redirect-addon.gif" alt="Addon Domain Redirect Configuration" style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }} />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Redirect">
    Once you have entered all the necessary details, click the <strong>Add</strong> button to create the redirect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Congratulations! You have successfully set up a redirect for your add-on domain. Visitors accessing your add-on domain will now be automatically sent to the specified destination URL.
