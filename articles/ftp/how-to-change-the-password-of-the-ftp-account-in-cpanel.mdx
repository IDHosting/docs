---
title: "Change the Password of the FTP Account in cPanel"
description: "Learn how to change the password for your specific FTP accounts within cPanel."
---

To change the password of a specific FTP account in cPanel, follow these instructions. Note that to change the password for the main root FTP account, you will need to change your cPanel account password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel account through your hosting provider's portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to FTP Accounts">
    In the **Files** section, click on **FTP Accounts**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cpanel-ftp-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel FTP Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change the FTP Account Password">
    Under the list of **FTP accounts**, locate the account for which you want to change the password and click on **Change Password**.
    <br />
    Enter your desired new password in the provided field. Alternatively, you can use the **Password Generator** to create a strong, random password.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/ftp-password-change.gif" 
        alt="FTP Password Change Interface"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the Password Change">
    Click on the **Change Password** button to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can now access your FTP account using the new password you have set.
