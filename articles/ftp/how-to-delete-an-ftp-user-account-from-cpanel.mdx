---
title: "Delete an FTP User Account From cPanel"
description: "Learn how to delete an FTP user account from your cPanel account."
---

This tutorial will guide you through the process of deleting an FTP user account from your cPanel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel dashboard by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to FTP Accounts">
    In the **Files** section, click on **FTP Accounts**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cpanel-ftp-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel FTP Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Delete for the FTP Account">
    Under the **FTP Accounts** list, locate the account you wish to delete and click on the **Delete** option next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/delete-ftp-account.gif" 
        alt="Delete FTP Account Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click on **Delete** once again to confirm and permanently remove the FTP account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Be cautious when deleting an FTP account. Do not select the <strong>Delete the User's Home Directory</strong> option unless you intend to permanently remove all data associated with that FTP user. This data will be unrecoverable without a backup.
</Warning>
