---
title: "Change the FTP User Quota in cPanel"
description: "This guide explains how to change the FTP user quota or set it to unlimited within your cPanel account."
---

Follow these steps to change the FTP quota or set it to Unlimited.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your cPanel account">
    Access your cPanel account using your provided credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to FTP Accounts">
    In the **Files** section, click on **FTP Accounts**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/cpanel-ftp-icon.gif" 
        alt="cPanel FTP Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the FTP Account and Change Quota">
    Under the **FTP Accounts** list, find the desired FTP user and click on **Change Quota**.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/change-ftp-quota.gif" 
        alt="Change FTP Quota Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Specify the New Quota">
    In the **FTP Quota** field, you can either specify a new quota in numerical words (e.g., `1024` for 1GB) or select **Unlimited** if you do not want to impose a limit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the Change">
    Click on the **Change Quota** button to save your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

We hope this guide is helpful.
