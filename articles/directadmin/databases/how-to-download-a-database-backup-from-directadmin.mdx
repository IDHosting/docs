---
title: "Download a Database Backup from DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to download a backup of your databases directly from your DirectAdmin control panel."
---

You can download backups of your databases from DirectAdmin for safekeeping or other purposes. This guide will walk you through the process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Databases">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **Databases**.
    Alternatively, you can type **Databases** into the **search bar** and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-databases-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Databases Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Database">
    From the list of your databases, click on **Manage** next to the database you wish to back up.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-mysql-db-manage-option.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Manage Database Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export the Database">
    Scroll down to the **Database Operations** section.
    Click either **Export as GZ** or **Export as SQL** to initiate the download.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-mysql-db-saveas.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Database Save As Options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the Backup File">
    Your browser will prompt you to download the backup file. Save this file to your computer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You have now successfully downloaded a database backup from DirectAdmin. It is recommended to keep this backup file in a safe place for future use, such as restoring your database if necessary.
