---
title: "Delete a Database in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to remove a database from your DirectAdmin control panel."
---

You can remove your database from the DirectAdmin control panel. It is recommended to create a backup of your database before deleting it.

## Steps to Delete a Database

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Databases">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **Databases**.
    Alternatively, you can type **Databases** in the **search bar** at the top of the page, and the option will appear. Click on it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-databases-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Databases Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Database">
    From the list of your databases, locate the one you wish to remove and click the **Delete** button next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-mysql-deletedb.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Delete Database Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Click **Yes** to confirm that you want to delete the selected database.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-mysql-confirmdel.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Confirm Database Deletion"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

The selected database should now be deleted from your DirectAdmin account.
