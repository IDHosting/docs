---
title: "Examine or Optimize a Database in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to examine and optimize your databases within the DirectAdmin control panel to improve performance."
---

This tutorial will guide you through the process of examining and optimizing a database using DirectAdmin. Optimizing your database can help it run more smoothly and improve overall performance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Begin by logging into your DirectAdmin account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Databases">
    Once logged in, locate the **Account Manager** section and click on **Databases**.
    <br />
    Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** at the top of the DirectAdmin interface. Type "Databases" and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-databases-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Databases icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Database to Manage">
    From the list of your databases, find the one you wish to examine or optimize and click the **Manage** button next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-mysql-db-manage-option.gif" 
        alt="Manage option for a MySQL database in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check or Optimize Database">
    Scroll down to the **Database Operations** section.
    <br />
    Click on **Check** to examine the database for errors or inconsistencies.
    <br />
    Click on **Optimize** to optimize the database for better performance.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-mysql-checkopt.gif" 
        alt="Check and Optimize buttons for MySQL database operations in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
    After the operation is complete, a report will be displayed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
