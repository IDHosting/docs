---
title: "Change an Email Account's Password in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to change your email account password using the DirectAdmin control panel."
---

Follow these steps to change your email password in DirectAdmin:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Access your DirectAdmin account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Accounts">
    In the **E-mail Manager** section, click on **E-mail Accounts**.
    Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** at the top of the page. Type "E-mail Accounts" and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-icons.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Email Account">
    In the **Email Accounts list**, find the email address for which you want to change the password. Look for the **three-dot icon** on the **right side** of the email address.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-plusbtn.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Account List with Three-Dot Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Password/Username">
    Click on the **three-dot icon** and select **Change Password/Username** from the dropdown menu.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-changepass.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Change Password Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter New Password">
    In the **Password field**, you can either type in a new password manually or click the **Generate Random Password** icon to create a strong, random password.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-passfrm.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Password Change Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click on the **Save** button to confirm the password change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your new password will be displayed in a popup box after saving. It is highly recommended to copy this password to a secure location immediately after generation.
</Note>
