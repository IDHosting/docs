---
title: "Change the Email Sending Limit in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to modify the email sending limits for your email accounts in DirectAdmin. Note that you cannot exceed the server administrator's set limit."
---

You can easily modify the email sending limit for your email accounts in DirectAdmin. However, please be aware that you cannot increase this limit beyond the maximum limit set by your server administrator.

## Steps to Change Email Sending Limit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Accounts">
    In the **E-mail Manager** section, click on **E-mail Accounts**. Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** and type "E-mail Accounts" to find and select the option.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-icons.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Accounts section"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Email Account">
    In the **Email Accounts list**, find the email address for which you want to change the limit. Look for a **three-dot icon** on the **right side** of the email address.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-plusbtn.gif" 
        alt="Email account list with three-dot icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change Limits Option">
    Click on the **three-dot icon** and select the **Change Limits** option from the dropdown menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-plusmail-lmt.gif" 
        alt="Change Limits option in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify Send Limit">
    In the **Send Limit** field, enter your desired limit (e.g., 200) or choose the **unlimited** option if it is available and suitable for your needs.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-email-sendlmt-quota.gif" 
        alt="Modifying the send limit in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click on the **Save** button to apply the new email sending limit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your email sending limit will now be updated.
