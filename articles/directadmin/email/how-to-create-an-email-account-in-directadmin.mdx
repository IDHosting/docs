---
title: "Create an Email Account in DirectAdmin"
description: "A guide on how to create a new email account using the DirectAdmin control panel."
---

Follow these steps to create a new email account in DirectAdmin.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Accounts">
    In the **E-mail Manager** section, click on **E-mail Accounts**. Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** and type "E-mail Accounts" to find and select the option.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-icons.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Accounts Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Account Creation">
    Click on the **Create Account** button, typically located in the top right corner of the E-mail Accounts page.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-createbtn.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Create Account Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email Details">
    Enter the desired username for your new email address (e.g., `my-first-email`). You can also set a password manually or click the **Generate Random Password** icon to create a strong, random password.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-create-frm.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Create Email Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Quota and Send Limit">
    Adjust the email **quota** (storage limit) and **Send Limit** as needed, or leave them at their default values. Finally, click **Create Account** to finalize the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Please ensure you copy the newly created email account details (username and password) to a safe and accessible place for future reference.
</Note>
