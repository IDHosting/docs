---
title: "Change Email Account Disk Quota in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to increase or modify the disk quota for your email accounts within the DirectAdmin control panel."
---

If your email disk quota has been reached and you need to increase or modify it, you can easily do so through your DirectAdmin account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Access your DirectAdmin account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Accounts">
    In the **E-mail Manager** section, click on **E-mail Accounts**. Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** and type "E-mail Accounts" to find and select the option.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-icons.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Accounts Icons"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Email Address">
    In the **Email Accounts list**, find the email address for which you want to change the quota. You will see a **three-dot icon** on the **right side** of the email address.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-plusbtn.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Account Plus Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Change Limits">
    Click on the **three-dot icon** and then choose **Change Limits** from the dropdown menu.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-plusmail-lmt.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Change Email Limits"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify Email Quota">
    In the **E-mail Quota** field, enter the new desired quota size. If an **unlimited** option is available and suitable for your needs, select it.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-email-quota-mdfy.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Modify Email Quota"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Save** button to apply your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your email account's quota limit will now be modified.
