---
title: "Access Your Email From DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to access your email account through DirectAdmin using two convenient methods."
---

You can access your email account from DirectAdmin using two methods.

## Method 1: Automatic Login

This method allows you to log in directly to your webmail from DirectAdmin without needing to re-enter your email credentials.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by navigating to its URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Accounts">
    In the **E-mail Manager** section, click on **E-mail Accounts**. Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** at the top of the page, type "E-mail Accounts", and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-icons.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Accounts section"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to Webmail">
    Under the **Email Account** list, locate the email address you wish to access and click the **Sign-in icon** next to it.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-sign-into-webmail-plus.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Sign-in icon for webmail"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow Popup Window">
    Your browser might prompt you to allow a popup window. Please confirm this action. You will then be redirected to your webmail interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Method 2: Using Email Username and Password

This method requires you to enter your full email address and password to log in to your webmail.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by navigating to its URL and entering your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Webmail">
    In the **Extra Features** section, click on your preferred webmail client (e.g., Roundcube). Alternatively, use the **search bar** at the top of the page, type "Webmail", and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-roundcube-icon.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Roundcube webmail icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    On the webmail login page, enter your full email address and your email account password.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-roundcube-login-pg.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin webmail login page"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login">
    Click the **Login** button to access your webmail.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once logged in through either method, you can manage your email account from the webmail reader.
