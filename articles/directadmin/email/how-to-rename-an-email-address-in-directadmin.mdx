---
title: "Rename an Email Address in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to rename an existing email address within your DirectAdmin control panel."
---

This guide will walk you through the process of renaming an email address in DirectAdmin.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Begin by logging into your DirectAdmin account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Email Accounts">
    In the **E-mail Manager** section, click on **E-mail Accounts**.
    Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** at the top of the DirectAdmin interface. Type "E-mail Accounts" and click on the result when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailaccounts-icons.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Accounts icons"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the Email Address">
    In the **Email Accounts list**, find the email address you wish to rename. To the right of the email address, you will see a **three-dot icon**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-plusbtn.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Email Account list with three-dot icon highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Change Password/Username">
    Click on the **three-dot icon** next to the email address. From the dropdown menu that appears, select **Change Password/Username**.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-changepass.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin change password/username option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename the Email Username">
    In the provided fields, modify the **Username** to your desired new email address name. You will also need to enter your email password.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-emailacct-userrename.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin form to rename email username and set password"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Click the **Save** button to confirm the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your email address has now been successfully renamed.
