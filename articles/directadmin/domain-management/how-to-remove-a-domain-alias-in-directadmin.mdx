---
title: "Remove a Domain Alias in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to remove unused domain aliases in your DirectAdmin control panel."
---

In this tutorial, you will learn how to remove a domain alias in DirectAdmin. This is useful when you no longer need the alias or want to clean up unused domain aliases.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Domain Pointers">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **Domain Pointers**.
    <br />
    Alternatively, you can type "Domain Pointers" in the search bar and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-domainpointer-icon.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Domain Pointers icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Domain Alias to Remove">
    Under the **Domain Pointers** section, locate and select the specific domain alias you wish to remove from the list.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-domainpointer-delete-alias.gif"
        alt="Selecting a domain alias to delete in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Domain Alias">
    After selecting the domain alias, click on the **Delete** button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your domain alias will now be successfully removed.
