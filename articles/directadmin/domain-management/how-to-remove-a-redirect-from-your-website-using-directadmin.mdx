---
title: "Remove a Redirect From Your Website Using DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to easily remove a website redirect configured through DirectAdmin."
---

You can easily remove a redirect you've previously set up in DirectAdmin by following these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Begin by logging into your DirectAdmin account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Site Redirection">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **Site Redirection**.
    Alternatively, you can use the **search bar** at the top of the page, type "Site Redirection", and click on the option when it appears.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-siteredirection-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Site Redirection Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Redirect to Remove">
    Under the **Local URL Path** column, find and select the specific redirect entry you wish to remove.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete the Redirect">
    Click the **Delete** button. You will be prompted to confirm this action. Click **Delete** again to finalize the removal.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-redirect-site-remove.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Redirect Site Remove Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once confirmed, the redirect will be successfully removed from your website.
