---
title: "Add a Subdomain in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to create a subdomain using your DirectAdmin control panel."
---

A subdomain is an additional part of your primary domain. For example, `blog.example.com` is a subdomain. This tutorial will guide you through the process of creating a subdomain in DirectAdmin.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Access your DirectAdmin account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Subdomain Management">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **Subdomain Management**.
    Alternatively, you can type **Subdomain Management** into the search bar and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-subdomain-management-icon.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Subdomain Management Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a New Subdomain">
    Click on the **Add Subdomain** button, typically located in the top right corner of the page.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-subdomain-create-btn.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Add Subdomain Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Subdomain Details">
    In the **Subdomain** field, enter the desired name for your subdomain (e.g., `blog`). Leave all other options at their default settings unless you have specific requirements.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-subdomain-add-blog.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Add Subdomain Blog Example"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Creation">
    Click on the **Add Subdomain** button to finalize the creation process.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon successful creation, you should see a confirmation message indicating that your subdomain has been created.
