---
title: "Remove Subdomain in DirectAdmin"
description: "A guide on how to remove a subdomain using the DirectAdmin control panel."
---

A subdomain is an additional part of your primary domain. Follow this tutorial to remove a subdomain in DirectAdmin.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Log in to your DirectAdmin account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Subdomain Management">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **Subdomain Management**.
    Alternatively, type **Subdomain Management** in the **search bar** and click on the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-subdomain-management-icon.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Subdomain Management Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Subdomain to Remove">
    Under the **Subdomain Name** column, select the subdomain you wish to remove.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-subdomain-remove-option.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Subdomain Remove Option"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Delete Subdomain">
    Click on the **Delete** button.
    If you also want to remove the associated directory contents, check the **Remove Directory Content** box before clicking **Delete**.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-subdomain-remove-confirm-opt.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin Subdomain Remove Confirmation Options"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your subdomain will now be removed.
