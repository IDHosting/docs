SpamAssassin is a powerful tool that helps filter unwanted emails and reduce spam in your inbox. This guide will walk you through the steps to enable it in your DirectAdmin control panel.

## Enabling SpamAssassin

Follow these steps to enable SpamAssassin:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to SpamAssassin Setup">
    In the **E-Mail Manager** section, click on **SpamAssassin Setup**.
    Alternatively, you can type **SpamAssassin** in the search bar at the top of the page, and the option will appear. Click on it.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-spamassasin-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin SpamAssassin icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable SpamAssassin">
    Click on the **Enable SpamAssassin** button.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-spamassasin-enable.gif" 
        alt="Enable SpamAssassin button in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>

    Once enabled, SpamAssassin will start filtering your emails. You can further configure its settings according to your preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
After enabling SpamAssassin, you can access its settings to adjust sensitivity levels, manage blacklists and whitelists, and review spam scores for incoming emails.
</Info>
