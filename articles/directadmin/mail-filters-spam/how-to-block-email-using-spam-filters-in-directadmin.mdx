---
title: "Block Email Using Spam Filters in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to block specific email addresses using the spam filters feature in DirectAdmin."
---

You can use spam filters in DirectAdmin to block emails from specific email addresses. This guide will walk you through the process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to SPAM Filters">
    In the **E-Mail Manager** section, click on **SPAM Filters**.
    Alternatively, you can type **SPAM Filters** in the **search bar** and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-spamfilters-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin SPAM Filters icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Email Blocking">
    Under the **Block By** section, choose **Email** from the **drop-down** menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email Address to Block">
    In the **Value** field, type the full email address you wish to block.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-block-spamemail.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin block spam email input field"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Blocking">
    Click the **Block** button to add the email address to your blocklist.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you need to block additional email addresses, simply repeat steps 3 through 5 for each address.
