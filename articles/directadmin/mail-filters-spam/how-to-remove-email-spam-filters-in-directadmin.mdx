---
title: "Remove Email Spam Filters in DirectAdmin"
description: "A guide on how to remove email spam filters from your DirectAdmin account."
---

You can easily remove unwanted email filters from your DirectAdmin account to manage your spam settings more effectively.

## Steps to Remove Email Spam Filters

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Begin by logging into your DirectAdmin account using your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to SPAM Filters">
    Once logged in, locate the **E-Mail Manager** section. Within this section, click on **SPAM Filters**.
    <Tip>If you can't find it immediately, use the **search bar** at the top of your DirectAdmin interface. Type "SPAM Filters" and click on the result when it appears.</Tip>
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-spamfilters-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin SPAM Filters icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Filters to Remove">
    On the SPAM Filters page, you will see a list of your current filters. Under the **Block By** section, select the specific filters you wish to remove by checking the corresponding boxes.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-remove-emailfilter.gif" 
        alt="Selecting email filters to remove in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    After selecting the filters, click the **Delete** button. A confirmation prompt will appear. Click **Delete** again to confirm the removal of the selected filters.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-filterremove-confirmation.gif" 
        alt="Confirmation prompt for deleting email filters in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your selected email filters will now be successfully removed from your DirectAdmin account.
