---
title: "Disable Apache SpamAssassin in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to disable Apache SpamAssassin through the DirectAdmin control panel."
---

This guide will walk you through the steps to disable Apache SpamAssassin in your DirectAdmin control panel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Access your DirectAdmin account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to SpamAssassin Setup">
    In the **E-Mail Manager** section, click on **SpamAssassin Setup**.
    Alternatively, you can use the search bar at the top of the DirectAdmin interface. Type **SpamAssassin** and click on the corresponding option when it appears.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-spamassasin-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin SpamAssassin icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable SpamAssassin">
    On the SpamAssassin setup page, click the **Disable SpamAssassin** button.

    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-spamassasin-disable.gif" 
        alt="Disable SpamAssassin button in DirectAdmin"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

SpamAssassin will now be successfully disabled for your account.
