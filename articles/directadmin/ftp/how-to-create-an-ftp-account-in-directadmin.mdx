---
title: "Create an FTP Account in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to create a separate FTP account in DirectAdmin for specific directory access."
---

You can access your FTP account using your DirectAdmin login details, which grants full access to your home directory. However, if you need to create an FTP user account for a specific directory, you can set up a separate FTP account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to FTP Management">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **FTP Management**. Alternatively, you can type "FTP Management" into the **search bar** and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-ftpmanagement-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin FTP Management Icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate FTP Account Creation">
    Click on the **Create FTP Account** button, typically located in the top right corner of the FTP Management page.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-createftp-accountbtn.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Create FTP Account Button"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure FTP Account Details">
    Enter the desired **FTP Username** (e.g., `firstuser`) and a strong password. Then, select the **Domain** or any other relevant option for this account.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-createftp-acform.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin Create FTP Account Form"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the FTP Account">
    Click the **Create** button to finalize the setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once created, your FTP account details will be displayed. It is recommended to copy these credentials to a secure location for future reference.
