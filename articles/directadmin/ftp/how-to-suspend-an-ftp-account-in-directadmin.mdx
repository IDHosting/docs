---
title: "Suspend an FTP Account in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to suspend an FTP account using the DirectAdmin control panel."
---

If your FTP account is not in use or you wish to suspend it, you can easily do so through your DirectAdmin control panel.

## Steps to Suspend an FTP Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to DirectAdmin">
    Access your DirectAdmin account by logging in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to FTP Management">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **FTP Management**.
    Alternatively, you can type **FTP Management** in the **search bar** and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-ftpmanagement-icon.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin FTP Management icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the FTP Account">
    Under the list of **FTP Accounts**, locate and choose the specific account you want to suspend.
    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/kb/da-ftpuser-suspend.gif"
        alt="DirectAdmin FTP user list with suspend option highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Suspend the Account">
    Click on the **Suspend** button for the selected FTP account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your FTP account will now be suspended. You can reactivate it at any time by clicking the **Unsuspend** option.
