---
title: "Remove an FTP Account in DirectAdmin"
description: "Learn how to remove additional FTP user accounts in DirectAdmin. Note that the main FTP account cannot be deleted."
---

You cannot delete the main FTP account. However, you can remove the additional FTP user accounts you created.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your DirectAdmin Account">
    Access your DirectAdmin control panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to FTP Management">
    In the **Account Manager** section, click on **FTP Management**.
    Alternatively, you can type **FTP Management** in the **search bar** and select the option when it appears.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-ftpmanagement-icon.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin FTP Management icon"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the FTP Account to Remove">
    Under the list of **FTP Accounts**, locate and choose the specific account you wish to remove.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-ftpuser-delete.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin FTP user list with delete option highlighted"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Click on the **Delete** button. A confirmation prompt will appear. Click **Delete** again to confirm the removal.
    <Frame>
      <img 
        src="/images/kb/da-ftpuser-delete-confim.gif" 
        alt="DirectAdmin FTP user delete confirmation dialog"
        style={{ width: '100%', height: 'auto' }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your FTP account will be successfully removed.
